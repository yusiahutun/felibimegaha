FIFA 20 TOTS Saudi League: Predictions, Release Time and Ultimate Team updates today

 Posted on May 22, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  681 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

FIFA 20 TOTSSF UPDATE - EA Sports has this evening announced the next Team of the Season So Far squad and as expected by the in-game notifications, its for the Saudi Pro League.

The team contains the likes of Giovinco with a massive 92 rated ST card as well as former Swansea striker Gomis with a 89 rated ST card as well.

More to follow, including full stats from EA Sports. For now you can see some of the stats for the players, just below, which is also being updated as we speak.

Original Story Follows - EA Sports are looking to do something they've never done before in yet another twist to the games famous Team of the Season promo.

So far EA Sports has released three promo squads. The Engish Football League, a Community Team voted for by players and most recently a Premier League squad.

It was assumed, by us included, that the next Promo would be for the games German leagues, with the FIFA 20 TOTSSF Bundesliga squad.

This is what EA has normally done.

However, it looks like players can look forward to yet another Team of the Season So Far squad announcement later today.

FIFA 20 players have spotted that the game has been displaying a countdown for the start of the Saudi TOTSSF league since Sunday afternoon.

You can see this below.

WHEN IS TOTSSF SAUDI LEAGUE OUT IN FIFA 20?

EA Sports in-game countdown ends at 6pm BST on Monday May 4th.

The first three Team of the Season So Far squads were also released at 6pm BST in the evening.

Whilst a Monday evening isn't the typical launch time for a new FIFA 20 promo, this is typically the time of day when the game might release a new promo. So it all makes sense.

Typically the release of a new FIFA 20 promo squad will see the previous promo removed from the games FUT packs. So on Friday evening, the FIFA 20 Premier League Team of the Season So Far squad replaced the EFL And Community picks in FUT packs.

However, with the Premier League having only been in packs for a handful of days, we think it's unlikely that the Saudi players will replace the Premier League stars.

If anything they'll simply be added to the FUT packs up until this Friday when we expect we'll see the release of the FIFA 20 TOTSSF Bundesliga squad.
