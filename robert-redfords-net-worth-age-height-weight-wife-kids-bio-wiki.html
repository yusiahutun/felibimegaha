Early Life

Charles Robert Redford Jr. was born on August 18, 1936, in Santa Monica, California, to his parents, Charles Robert Redford Sr. and Martha W. Redford. His family later settled in Van Nuys, California.

He completed his schooling from Van Nuys High School in 1954. Following that, he went to the University of Colorado, located in Boulder. He later went to Brooklyn’s Pratt Institute and also went to New York City’s American Academy of Dramatic Arts.
