Rocky Driving School customizes its course and fees to match individual needs and services. As such, the institution offers a variety of flexible services to its customers. On average, a course may take between three to four weeks. For people with tight schedules, Rocky allows you to choose 2 to 3 hours a day convenient for you. Rocky Driving school is open from Monday to Saturday from 8.00 am. The weekday classes go up to 7.00pm while the Saturday class ends at 1 pm. Also, Rocky Driving school has a crash program for people with urgent needs. The course covers areas such as basic mechanics, advanced and long-distance driving.

Rocky Driving School Fees

Rocky segments its classes into different groups each attracting varying fees.

Class A

Class A is a group of people interested in learning how to ride motorbikes. For 15 classes you will pay amounts from Kshs 8000.

Class B

Specially made for people learning how to drive a saloon car. The charge for this course is from Kshs 14,500.

Class C

If you want to know how to steer heavy commercial vehicles, this is the class for you. The rate for this class begins from Kshs 16,000.

Class F

The class is personalized to meet the needs of aspiring automatic or paraplegic drivers. The total cost for this package starts from Kshs 14,500.
