There are only a few celebrities in Hollywood who can maintain a steady career for a long time, and Don Diamont is one of them. He has been a heartthrob since the nineties through soap operas such as 'The Young and the Restless and 'The Bold and the Beautiful.'

Don Diamont's Wife is 'Seinfeld' Actress Cindy Ambuehl

Don Diamont and Cindy Ambuehl have been married for the past eight years. The couple married at the Chèvre d'or Hotel in Eze, France, on June 12, 2012.

Don Diamont with his wife Cindy on their wedding day.  SOURCE: Don Diamont's Instagram

It is the second marriage for both Diamond and his wife. The actor was previously married to Rachel Braun. Diamont and Braun were married in 1993 until their divorce in 2002.

Don Diamont Has Seven Sons 

Don Diamont shares four sons (Alexander, aka Zander Diamont, Sasha Braun Diamont, Luca Diamont, and Lauren Braun Diamont) with his ex-wife Rachel Braun. Point to note that Lauren and Sasha were born to his ex-wife Rachel from her previous relationship but became Diamont after Don's marriage to Rachel. Diamont also started raising his sister Bette's son Drew Mazur after her passing and is now a member of the Diamont family. Similarly, after his marriage to his wife Cindy, the duo has two sons twins Anton Ambuehl and Davis Ambuehl who were born in 2003. 

Don Diamont with his seven sons. SOURCE: Don Diamont's Instagram

Don has shared his experience of raising seven sons in a book titled My Seven Sons and How We Raised Each Other: (They Only Drive Me Crazy 30% of the Time). The book was published on May 29, 2018, and is available for purchase at various sites.

Update 2023: Don Diamont is a Grandfather!

In a recent Instagram post in August 2023, Don joyfully shared the news of soon becoming a grandfather. His son Alexander and daughter-in-law Audrey are expecting, bringing happiness and anticipation to the Diamont family.

Don Diamont's Net Worth is in Millions

Don Diamont's net worth as of 2020 is reported to be $4 million. He primarily earned this fortune from his successful career as a soap actor.

Diamont, who started his career as a model, entered the world of soap in 1984 with the show Days of Our Lives. Since then, he has worked in other popular soap operas, including The Young and the Restless and The Bold and the Beautiful.

Diamont's other sources of income include the sales of books and brand promotions. He has been a longtime spokesperson for Morris Home Furnishings in Dayton, Ohio.
