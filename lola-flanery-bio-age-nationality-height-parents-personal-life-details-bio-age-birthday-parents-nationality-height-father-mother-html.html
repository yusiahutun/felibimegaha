American budding actress Lola Flanery carved her career in the realm of the entertainment industry, and she is widely applauded for her acting talent.  After being a celebrity, fans are not only intrigued to know about Lola's professional life; they are even enthusiastic to know about her personal life too.

If you curious to know about Lola Flanery's personal life, then lets dive into to know more!

Lola Flanery's Bio

Actress Lola Flanery celebrates her birthday on 26th May every year; and was born in 2005 in the United States. The actress possesses an American nationality and belongs to a white ethnicity. Lola stands at the height of 1.5 meters (4 feet 9 inches).

Talking about her parents, her mother, Sacha Grierson runs a bakery in Toronto named 'Eat My Words.' Moreover expressing her fondness for her father, Lola shared a throwback picture with her father on the occasion of Father's Day via Instagram back on 18th June 2017.

Lola Flanery with her father (Photo: Lola Flanery's Instagram)

Although Lola has least flaunted her parents in social media platforms, she mentions her onscreen family time and again! Back on 3rd November 2016, she shared a picture of her on-screen sister and herself. Lola portrays the role of a sister in the movie Home Again, and in the picture, the onscreen siblings are acting silly.

Lola Flanery with her on-screen sister Eden (Photo: Lola Flanery's Instagram account)  

Lola, who is at the age of 12, made her acting debut in 2015 from holiday telemovie Last Chance for Christmas. Later, in the year 2016, she greeted the screen with the role of a younger version of the character, Sadie Mitchell in ‘Saide’s Last Days on Earth.’ Moreover, she has portrayed the role of Alice in the thriller movie ‘Lavender’ along with Abbie Cornish and Dermot Mulroney. She also worked in 2016 film named Trauma.

Lola is best known for her performance as Isabel in the comedy and romance based film Home Again released in 2017. She essayed the role of Isabel alongside Reese Witherspoon, Michael Sheen, and Lake Bell. Furthermore, she made her appearance in the TV show named The Mist in which she portrayed the role of Lila DeWitt. Lola also acted in the 2018 Science fiction and mystery based TV series The 100 as Madi.
