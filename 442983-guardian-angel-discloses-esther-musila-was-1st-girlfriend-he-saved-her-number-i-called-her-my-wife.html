Guardian Angel's wife, Esther Musila, has said that God played the biggest part in their relationship.

During an interview with Dennis Njau on The Goldmine Show on his YouTube channel, the couple revealed they fell in love the first time they set eyes on each other.

"We have been married from the first day we met," the mom of three said.

Musila revealed that the Hadithi hitmaker saved her as 'my wife' on his phonebook after their first date.
