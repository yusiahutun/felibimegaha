Clay Walker, Wife Welcome a Baby Boy!

 Posted on July 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  247 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Clay Walker and his wife, Jessica, have welcomed their fourth child, a son named Ezra Stephen Walker.

Walker revealed the news via social media on Friday (Nov. 3), writing, "Our newest angel!! Ezra Stephen Walker has arrived! He and Mama are healthy and home:) Blessed...thank you for your prayers always."

The singer did not provide further details.

"Jessica and I are beyond excited to be expecting a new addition to our loving family," Walker said in a press release the following day. "There is no greater challenge nor achievement than being a father and I am beyond blessed and humbled to be one six times over. 2017 is certainly going to be a great year filled with our family growing and new music and I am ecstatic about both!"

Walker and Jessica have made a habit of high-profile pregnancy reveals. They revealed they were expecting in 2008 when they showed up at a red carpet event with Jessica visibly pregnant, then confirmed the news via Walker’s fan club newsletter. In 2013, they revealed they were expecting their third child on the ACMs red carpet. The couple welcomed their third child, a son named Elijah Craig Walker, in June of 2013.

The couple have two other children together; their son William is 9, and they also have a 7-year-old daughter, Mary. Walker also has two teenage daughters from a previous marriage.
