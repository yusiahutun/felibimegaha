Daisy Jones &amp; The Six star Suki Waterhouse nailed her Coachella look - see photos!

 Posted on June 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  269 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Suki Waterhouse wore a simple but stylish outfit to Coachella….

    If you've been obsessed with all the Coachella looks over the past few days, we're about to show you one of the most wearable yet - cue Suki Waterhouse - aka Karen Sirko from the hit Amazon Prime show Daisy Jones & The Six. 

    Attending the Revolve party at Coachella, Suki looked amazing in a pair of denim jeans, a crop top and a super wearable (and on-trend) blue and white striped Solid & Striped button-down shirt. She accessorised with an Olga Berg bag and Seychelles heels - all from Revolve.

    PHOTOS: All the best dressed stars at Coachella weekend 1

    The 31-year-old Brit wasn't alone, she was in attendance with her co-star Camila Morrone who wore a James Perse T-shirt,  Re/Done Jeans and a pair of Bottega Veneta sunglasses.

    The duo really are a force to be reckoned with in the style stakes. Suki shared a photo gallery from Coachella, captioning it: "Bring your bestie to the desert and not look like a couple challenge."
