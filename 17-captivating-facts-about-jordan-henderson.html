Rising Star: From Sunderland to Liverpool

Jordan Henderson started his professional football career at Sunderland before joining Liverpool in 2011.

Premier League Title Winner: 2019-2020 Season

Henderson played a crucial role in leading Liverpool to their first Premier League title in 30 years during the 2019-2020 season.

FIFA Club World Cup: Triumph in 2019

Henderson lifted the FIFA Club World Cup in 2019, with Liverpool defeating Flamengo in the final.

UEFA Champions League Winner: 2018-2019 Season
