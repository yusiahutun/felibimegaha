She's an Academy Award-winning actress, but also a proud wife and mother to three children, as her back tattoo reveals.

And Erin Brockovich star Julia Roberts was flashing her inking when she was spotted on a family vacation in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico over the weekend, lounging by the pool and flashing her stunning physique.

Roberts, 52, also flashed her lower back tattoo, which displays the names of her three children with cinematographer husband Danny Moder: twins Phinneus and Hazel, both 15, and younger son Henry, 12, alongside her spouse's initials. 

Thousand-watt smile: Julia Roberts was spotted in family vacation mode in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico over the weekend

The names of her children are arranged around Moder's initials, which Julia revealed in a 2014 visit to The Ellen DeGeneres Show she got before their 2002 wedding.

When asked if she has any tattoos, Julia was relatively bashful as she told the host: 'Well, some things I can tell you the answer to and some things I’ll tell you later. I have all my children’s names and my husband’s initials.'
