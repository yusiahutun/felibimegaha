Kendall Jenner Welcomes Summer in a Teeny Red Thong Bikini

 Posted on May 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  303 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Summer just began, and Kendall Jenner is already giving us beach style inspiration.

The supermodel shared a post on Instagram today featuring several sexy shots of her posing in a red-hot bikini. The two-piece swimsuit featured an asymmetric one-shoulder top and a teeny thong. In the photos, she is posing on a white bed in nothing but the bikini.

The look is from her latest FWRD edit. Since September 2021, Jenner has been the creative director for the brand, and has modeled her favorite styles all over social media. In March, for example, she shared some sultry photos of herself also posing on a white bed, but that time wearing a semi-sheer white tank dress and black leather knee-high boots.

Jenner is also no stranger to posting bikini shots on her Instagram. Just last month, she shared photos of herself soaking up the sun and lounging on a white-sand beach while wearing a stringy black triangle bikini top and matching bikini bottoms. She accessorized the look minimally, opting for just a cherry-red baseball cap from Ralph Lauren. She also carried a cream ceramic bottle in the shape of an 8, one of the products from her 818 tequila line.

She took in the sun in another bikini weeks earlier—a frilly white two-piece by Heavy Manners with a dainty yellow, purple, and green floral pattern. Her minimalist accessories included an 818 baseball cap in a burnt orange shade and brown oval sunglasses.
