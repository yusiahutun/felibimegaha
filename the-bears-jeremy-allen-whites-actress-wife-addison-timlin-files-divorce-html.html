The Bear's Jeremy Allen White's actress wife Addison Timlin files for divorce

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  392 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Jeremy Allen White's wife has filed for divorce from him after more than three years of marriage and two children together.

Actress Addison Timlin filed paperwork to end her marriage to White, the star of the hit series The Bear and Shameless, on Thursday, according to TMZ.

Timlin, 31, and White, 32, married at the Beverly Hills courthouse in October 2019. They share daughters Ezer, four, and Dolores, two.

White even gave his wife a shout-out when receiving a Golden Globe just four months ago: 'I love you deep in my bones. Thank you for all that you do, thank you for everything that you've done.'

What exactly led to the split remains unknown.

White and Timlin were just teenagers when they first met on the set of the 2008 film Afterschool, according to Us Weekly.  

It's not entirely clear when exactly White and Timlin began dating, but it appears their romance may have begun around 2013.

Timlin gave an interview at the time revealing she had entered a new romance - but refrained from saying who the lucky suitor was.

'I've got a sweetheart who I will not disclose, but, yes, he'd be the guy. We have a nice foundation from being friends for a long period of time, and then it took a hard right turn into romance,' she told Harper's Bazaar.

They married in 2019, a year after welcoming daughter Ezer, and Coldplay singer Chris Martin and his girlfriend Dakota Johnson both attended, serving as witnesses. 

Last year, White shed light on his home life during an interview with NPR. 

Asked what television shows he was watching, he replied: 'I have a 3-year-old and a 1-year-old at home. 

'When my wife and I put them to bed, we don’t want to think about anything.'

'So we're into the housewives. We're into "Below Deck." That's our speed right now,' he added.

Addison has had roles on Law & Order, Californication, and most recently, American Horror Story.

White found fame on Shameless before finding even more success as the star of Hulu's The Bear.
