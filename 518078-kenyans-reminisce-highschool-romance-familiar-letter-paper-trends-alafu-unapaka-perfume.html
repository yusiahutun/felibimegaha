A familiar paper has taken Kenyans down memory lane.

Remember your high school love letter?

The world has pretty much changed, with the means of communication being primarily electronic.

Kenyans were left nostalgic when a familiar paper was shared by a T6witter user @SokoAnalyst.

The paper, associated with high school romance, was well decorated with pink flowers on its periphery.

The photo of the paper caused collective nostalgia and ignited a yearning for familiar times among netizens.

It evoked emotions of innocence, young love and the nervous excitement of confessing one's feelings.

Netizens shared their various experiences, with some disclosing how they would enlist peers with good handwriting to write the letters.

Others said they sprayed the letter for a pleasing smell and to invoke nostalgia for their loved ones.
