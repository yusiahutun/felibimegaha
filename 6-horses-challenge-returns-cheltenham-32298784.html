The Silicon Tipster – bet365 Supercomputer predicts Cheltenham 2024 winners

What is the Supercomputer and how does it work?

bet365 have activated their horse racing supercomputer to crunch the data and determine which horse has the best chance of winning the 2024 Gold Cup, alongside all of the other races at the festival.

We collected comprehensive data via Irishracing.com on the past winners and contenders of the Cheltenham Gold Cup since 1990. The next step was to determine the importance of the horses’ characteristics, in terms of what is advantageous for the race in question. This was done by consulting domain experts, and the characteristics were further classified as ‘numerical’ and ‘categorical’ features.

Using the algorithm mentioned above, we extracted the characteristics of all winning horses at Cheltenham Festival, to determine the characteristics of a ‘typical winning horse’ at the race. Once they were extracted, we used the median values as a representation of the characteristics of a typical winner at each race.

We extracted the relevant data for all horses competing at the 2024 Cheltenham Festival and then assigned similarity scores. The closer the similarity score to 1, the higher the winning probability of the particular horse.

Gold Cup predicted winner…

The supercomputer’s simulations, interestingly, favour Fastorslow who has a similarity score of 0.9854. The second favourite is Irish horse Shishkin and closely following it is Gerri Colombe from France. Defending champions Galopin des Champs is down as the tenth favourite, as per the supercomputer, which while a surprising prediction, shows that it’s not just statistics that make a winner.

The Methodology

● Data on past winners was collected via Irishracing.com and then analysed to determine the characteristics of a typical winning horse

● Data is from 1990 to 2023

● That data for the characteristics of a typical winning horse was then compared to the characteristics of the provisional field competing in 2024

● We assigned similarity scores to the field competing in 2024 - the higher the similarity score, the higher the chance of winning

● In case of missing data, median values from historical data were used for representation
