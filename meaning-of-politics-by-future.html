Meaning of Politics by Future

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  450 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The Future song titled 'Politics' delves into the complexities of personal struggles, the realities of society, and the everlasting pursuit of success. With its introspective lyrics and raw emotions, this song explores the theme of navigating through a tumultuous world to find one's place in it.One standout lyric in the song is "That ghetto keep my heart pumpin', baby you ain't perfect." Here, Future acknowledges the dynamic nature of the environment he comes from, highlighting its impact on his identity and drive. The theme of resilience emerges, as he embraces the challenges he faces and uses them as fuel to keep pushing forward.Another significant line is "I can see the grass and the snakes, and they thirsty." In this lyric, Future exposes the harsh realities of a society where individuals exploit others for personal gain. This highlights themes of betrayal, envy, and the constant need to stay vigilant. Future's ability to discern between genuine people and those harboring ulterior motives becomes paramount to his success."I put all my soul in the grind, and it's worth it" is yet another impactful line that resonates deeply. It speaks to the theme of determination, sacrifice, and the relentless pursuit of dreams. Future recognizes that success doesn't come easily and requires pouring oneself entirely into the grind. This lyric empowers listeners to embrace hard work and suggests that the payoff will ultimately be fulfilling.The lyric "My God given gift through the struggle" highlights the contrasting ability to persevere even in the face of hardship. Here, Future acknowledges the blessings and talents he possesses, recognizing that they have helped him overcome obstacles. The theme of self-awareness and cultivating resilience stands out, emphasizing the importance of recognizing and harnessing one's strengths.Finally, the line "Poison on these dollars in this collar shit, politics" sheds light on the corrupting influence of money and power in politics. Future draws a parallel between the toxicity of politics and the negative impacts of wealth on society. This lyric suggests that the pursuit of material wealth can sometimes detract from one's genuine passions and values.The overarching theme of 'Politics' is the multifaceted journey of self-discovery, navigating the challenging terrain of society, and remaining true to oneself amidst external pressures. Through the exploration of themes like resilience, betrayal, determination, self-awareness, and the corrupting influence of wealth, Future crafts a deeply introspective and thought-provoking narrative.The song encourages listeners to reflect on their own struggles and recognize the importance of staying true to one's values and passions. By addressing the complexities of personal growth and the harsh realities of the world, 'Politics' acts as both a mirror and a guiding light for listeners, reminding them to persevere and find purpose in their own journey.
