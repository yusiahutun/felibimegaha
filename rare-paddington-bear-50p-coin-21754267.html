Rare Paddington Bear 50p coin at St Paul's Cathedral selling on eBay for 3,000

 Posted on February 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  346 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Are you a rare coin collector?

A “very rare” 50p coin with Paddington Bear at St Paul's Cathedral is currently being sold for an absolute fortune on eBay.

The seller is flogging the small bit of change for a whopping £3,000 which has since had over 200 watchers on the page.

It was brought into circulation in 2019, along with the Paddington Bear coin series which was released in 2018 by The Royal Mint.

Some editions have already been flogging for thousands, with Paddington Bear at Tower of London on eBay for £6,000.

The 50p coin commemorating the loveable character in children’s book has sold for 600 times its face value in the past.

Meanwhile, the seller on eBay had to re-list the “very rare” 50p coin due to time-wasting and will only “answer serious questions” about the change.
