ABC journalist Sarah Ferguson, 52, talks body confidence as she reveals she prefers bikini's

 Posted on July 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  423 words  |  Aldo Pusey

ABC Four Corners presenter, Sarah Ferguson has led the charge on a number of high-profile investigations into animal cruelty, Australia's domestic violence crisis and Sydney's Lindt cafe siege.

And on Sunday the Gold Walkley award winner tackled another topic close to many people's hearts- finding body confidence as a woman over 50. 

Speaking to Stellar, the ABC journalist confessed: 'When I was young I used to admire women in their 50s in bikinis and now I am one!'

'I’ve been on the full journey back to the one-piece, but it didn’t do me any favours. I’ll stick with a bikini forever, I reckon,' she added.
