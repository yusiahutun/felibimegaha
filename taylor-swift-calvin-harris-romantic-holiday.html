Taylor Swift and Calvin Harris enjoy romantic getaway after celebrating 1 year anniversary

 Posted on June 2, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  463 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Taylor Swift and Calvin Harris appear even more in love than ever after celebrating their first anniversary together. The couple have continued their celebrations by jetting away on an exotic holiday together, which they have been documenting for their fans on social media.

The Shake it Off singer became the envy of fans by sharing a series of idyllic snaps and videos from their romantic getaway, including an amusing clip showing her bouncing on a huge inflatable in the middle of the sea before jumping into the crystal clear waters.

Taylor Swift and Calvin Harris are on a romantic holiday together

"That hurt a lot!" she can be heard saying, while Calvin laughs in the background.

Another photo showed the loved-up pair sharing a kiss on the beach, and Taylor also wrote her initials into the sand along with "AW" – the initials of Calvin's real name, Adam Wiles.

"Taylor Swift + Adam Wiles," she simply captioned the image.

Calvin and Taylor shared photos from the getaway with their fans

Calvin also couldn't resist sharing a few holiday photos with his 5.1 million fans, including an image of Taylor posing in a bikini. The DJ didn't caption the images but was inundated with comments from fans who described Calvin and Taylor as the "sweetest couple ever!" Another referenced the transformation Calvin has undergone over the past few years, writing that he has "come along from walking down the streets of Glasgow".

The power couple's tropical escape comes just over a week after they marked their first anniversary with a special cake and lavish gifts. Taylor revealed that Calvin had bought her a heart-shaped locket engraved with the date 3.6.15 – the date the couple started dating last year – and told fans: "One year down!"

The couple wrote their initials in the sand

Their romance is clearly going from strength-to-strength; supportive Calvin recently shared a sweet snapshot on Instagram after Taylor won the Grammy for Best Album, which he captioned: "Congratulations to my beautiful girlfriend. "In fact, things are going so well for the pair that Calvin has "politely declined" the chance to work with Taylor, because he doesn't want to jeopardise their romance by making their relationship professional.

Calvin couldn't resist sharing photos of his girlfriend Taylor

A source told The Sun on Sunday newspaper: "Calvin's next single is ready but the label is keen for him to get a big female US star to collaborate on it. They know he attracts amazing names because everyone wants to work with him.

"They floated the idea of him working with Taylor but he politely declined it. "Calvin has seen how sour things can turn when working with someone he's dating. He is very serious about Taylor and doesn't want to rock the boat. He's smitten."
