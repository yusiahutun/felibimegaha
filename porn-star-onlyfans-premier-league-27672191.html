Meet the porn stars and OnlyFans babes cheering on Premier League clubs this season

The Premier League 2022/23 season is underway after a new-look Arsenal beat Crystal Palace on Friday evening at Selhurst Park.

And as the English top flight kicks into full swing over the weekend, there will be some familiar faces throwing their support behind their sides on social media. Of course, there will be the familiar celebrity fans, but Daily Star Sport are looking at the more X-rated options.

From die-hard OnlyFans creators to porn stars who have a soft spot for a certain side - Daily Star Sport profiles which x-rated fans belong to which club.

Elle Brooke - Manchester City

Elle Brooke has rocketed into something of a social media phenomenon thanks to her Twitter and OnlyFans pages.

And Brooke hit mainstream headlines when she appeared on a YouTube/TikTok boxing card where she was supposed to face Chelsea fan Astrid Wett (more on her later). However, when Wett pulled out Brooke still fought and beat AJ Bunker.

It's on her huge social media platform that Brooke regularly throws her support behind her beloved Manchester City.

Astrid Wett - Chelsea

Brooke's opponent, Astrid Wett, also has a huge online presence - and alongside her fanatic support online for Chelsea, Wett is also a regular at Stamford Bridge.

Last season, as the Blues geared up for their FA Cup final against Liverpool, Wett teased: "When Chelsea are about to win another trophy. 5th video might be on its way you know."

The blonde bombshell was referring to her fifth video on Pornhub - where she currently has four, apparently.

Emily Black - Manchester United

OnlyFans babe and YouTuber Emily Black will be firmly behind Erik ten Hag during his maiden campaign at Manchester United.

While Wett and Brooke prefer to throw their support behind their clubs with a barrage of tweets - Black prefers to sport her United shirt in saucy snaps. One Instagram snap shows the brunette wearing the United shirt with a pair of tight shorts.

Black has a mega 497,000 likes on the subscription site.

Mia Khalifa - West Ham

American-Lebanese sensation Mia Khalifa rocketed to fame after just three months in the adult industry in 2014, becoming the most viewed performer on Pornhub in just two months.

However, the star bizarrely opted to throw her support behind West Ham, and she revealed: "I watched Green Street hooligans when I was young and ever since then I've loved West Ham.

"They (fans in Green Street) were just so rough around the edges and a little badass, and also I love the song, Bubbles."

Poppy Evans - Liverpool

Back to OnlyFans creators, and Liverpool have their own superfan on the subscription site in the form of Poppy Evans.

Evans once offered fans a free subscription to her site if they could guess the correct scoreline for the Reds clash with City in November 2019. And her support didn't end there as she revealed to the Daily Star earlier this year that she was confident of a quadruple.

However, Evans only has eyes for one star: “Andy Robertson – he's so hot."

Rhiannon Blue - Leeds United

Leeds United have one of the most fanatical fanbases on the planet - so of course, they have at least one x-rated fan.

The stunning blonde regularly models in a Peacocks shirt - pulled up enough to show off her sizeable assets. Blue is from Leeds, and she swapped a £14,000-per-year nanny job for a gig on the subscription site.

Blue will be right behind Jesse Marsch as he looks to step out of the sizeable shadow cast by Marcelo Bielsa at Elland Road.
