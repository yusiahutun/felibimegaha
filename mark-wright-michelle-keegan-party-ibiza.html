Michelle Keegan and Mark Wright party in Ibiza for her Lipsy launch

 Posted on July 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  417 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

    Michelle Keegan and Mark Wright made the most of the bank holiday weekend with a fun-filled trip to Ibiza. The HELLO! Online blogger was over on the White Isle to promote her new Lipsy clothing range, but mixed business with pleasure as she partied at the Ibiza Spray party in collaboration with Lipsy at Ocean Beach Ibiza.

    Wearing an outfit from her collection, Michelle looked as glamorous as ever in her black fringed bandeau top and tie-dye skirt, topped off with an oversized hat and gold body art from boutique Kiss My Fairy. She cosied up to her husband, who was dressed for the summer weather in a white t-shirt and mint green shorts.

    Michelle Keegan and Mark Wright partied in Ibiza on Sunday

    While Mark got behind the decks and played a DJ set at the Ibiza Spray party, Michelle took the opportunity to catch up with her former co-star Brooke Vincent and sister-in-law Jessica Wright.

    She also changed into a second outfit from her Lipsy collection – a pom pom floral co-ord set – as she mingled with the crowd which included runner up Fleur East.

    Brooke Vincent and Jessica Wright joined Michelle in Ibiza

    Michelle had discussed her exciting plans for the weekend in her most recent blog for HELLO! Online, writing: "I am heading to the white island of Ibiza for a weekend of work and play with Lipsy London… We are going to Ocean Club on the Sunday for a fun pool party and I am doing a photoshoot over there too.

    "I can't wait! Some of my friends are meeting me there too so it will be a weekend to remember I am sure."

    Michelle showed off designs from her Lipsy collection

    The 28-year-old and her husband have been enjoying a series of fun summer getaways over the past few weeks including a trip to the States where they both had business meetings with the suggestion of a possible move to fulfil their career ambitions in the future.
