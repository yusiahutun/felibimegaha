Beyoncé unveiled a new cowboy-chic look when she joined her family at the 2024 Grammy Awards ceremony on Sunday.

The 42-year-old hitmaker — who skipped the red carpet — showcased her Western style while rocking an eye-catching blond wig, even though she wasn't nominated in any categories this year.

She put on a loved-up display with her husband Jay Z, 54 — who was honored with the Dr. Dre Global Impact Award at the ceremony at the Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles — when they shared a sweet kiss.

During his acceptance speech, the furious rapper bashed the awards show for failing to ever award Beyoncé the coveted album of the year award, despite her multiple nominations in the category and her record-breaking number of Grammy wins. The award ultimately went to Taylor Swift, who broke the record for most album of the year wins.

Beyoncé and Jay Z also brought along their daughter Blue Ivy, 12, who looked shockingly mature, despite her dad's joke about her 'sippy cup' at the start of his speech.

The beaming beauty was seen posing for photos with some of the evening's big nominees, including the country–pop crossover singer Kacey Musgraves and Victoria Monét, who won best new artist at the ceremony, as well as best R&B album and best engineered album, non-classical for her LP Jaguar II.
