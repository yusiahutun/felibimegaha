Chip Ganassi's bio

Chip was born on May 24th, 1958, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, United States. His birth name is Floyd Ganassi Jr. He was born to Marie Moia and Floyd Ganassi. Unfortunately, not much about his early life is in the limelight.

Floyd Ganassi Jr. went to Duquesne University. He graduated with a degree in finance in 1982. He also received an Honorary Doctorate from Carnegie Mellon University in 2011.

Profession

Chip Ganassi is a businessman, former racing driver and current team owner. He is also a member of the Motorsports Hall of Fame of America. He attended the Bob Bondurant Driving School in 1977.

Chip started his CART (Champ Car) racing career in 1982 upon graduating from Duquesne. He achieved his top sportscar result in the 1986 Kodak Copies 500 at Watkins Glen.
