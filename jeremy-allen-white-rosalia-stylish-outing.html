Jeremy Allen White and Rosala Coordinated in Stylish Black Jackets for Casual Outing

 Posted on March 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  299 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Jeremy Allen White and Rosalía are quickly proving themselves to be Hollywood’s It couple of 2024.

The pair were recently spotted on the streets of New York City, where they matched in nearly all-black ensembles. Rosalía sported a black zip-up jacket with oversize sleeves, which she layered over a black miniskirt with slits and a pair of tights, while also donning strappy stiletto kitten heels. The “Despechá” singer added a few pops of pastel pink, with a button-up underneath her jacket and a scarf with fringe hems, which she carried in her hand.

For White’s part, he wore a black zip-up top stitched with thin white stripes across the chest and hemlines, which he layered under a black leather bomber jacket and paired with straight-leg pants cuffed at the bottom. The Iron Claw actor accessorized with a black beanie and brown leather lace-up boots.

TheImageDirect.com

Rumors of the pair’s relationship first circulated in October, after they were spotted having dinner together and at a screening of the movie Wild Things. Their relationship was confirmed a month later when a source told Us Weekly, “Rosalía and Jeremy Allen White have recently started dating … They started out as just friends, but things have turned romantic recently.”

White was previously married to actor Addison Timlin, from 2019 until their separation in May 2023. They share two children together: daughters Ezer, five, and Dolores, three. Meanwhile, Rosalía was engaged to singer Rauw Alejandro, until they ended their relationship this past summer.
