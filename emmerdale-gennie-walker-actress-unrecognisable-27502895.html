Sian announced her departure from the show in May 2013, deciding that five years is a good time to leave.

Gennie's exit was kept secret until the episode broadcast July 25 2013, where the popular villager was suffocated by bad boy Cameron Murray (Dominic Power) following a car crash.

Despite Gennie dying in July, she later appeared in a pre-recorded video on the episode broadcast on August 15 2013.

Sian appeared in the now axed continuing drama for four months.
