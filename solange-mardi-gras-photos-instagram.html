When your style is as colorful (and as structural, eclectic and effortless) as Solange's, dressing for Mardi Gras is no hassle. The singer celebrated Fat Tuesday in New Orleans with her husband, equipped with a perfectly vibrant wardrobe and eye makeup for the occasion.

While most opt for beads and masks in the shades of purple, green and gold, Knowles took another route and went one step further. She placed peacock feathers in her curly updo and adorned her forehead with adhesive body jewelry that went down to her eyelids and upper cheekbones. Her outfit was equally striking, with a ruched, off-the-shoulder neon pink top.

Look at this mother of a makeup job.

Her look the day before was equally colorful. On Lundi Gras, she sported a breezy tangerine dress and matching statement earrings, with a baby pink bucket bag, yellow socks and two-tone Mary Janes.

Solange / Instagram Stories

Her eye makeup that day was noteworthy too—she simply drew red lines on her brow bones, inner eye corners and lower lash line.
