Colony Reeves is set to appear as one of the stars in Netflix’s Selling Tampa. But long before Netflix and the reality TV world found her, Colony was already a high-end real estate agent in Florida’s mouth-watering real estate scene.

Get to know this beauty with high-class business skills in this article. Find out about her role in Selling Tampa, about her romantic partner, and some other information like her age, net worth, height, etc.

Colony Reeves On Selling Tampa

Selling Tampa is the spinoff of Netflix’s other real-estate-based reality show Selling Sunset. Selling Tampa is created by Adam DiVello, who is also the creator of Selling Sunset.

While Selling Sunset followed the staff of The Oppenheim Group in California, Selling Tampa will follow the realtors of Allure Realty in Tampa, Florida. Allure Realty is founded and operated by Sharelle Rosado and is comprised of all-women, all-black realtors.

Apart from Colony Reeves and Sharelle Rosado, other realtors in Allure Realty (and cast members of Selling Tampa) are Juawana Colbert, Anne-Sophie Petit-Frere, Tennille Moore, Rena Upshaw-Frazier, Karla Giorgio, and Alexis C. Williams.

Colony announced her part in Selling Tampa on Instagram on 23 November 2021 and said that she could not wait for everyone to see “what’s in the store on Dec 15th”. And yes, Selling Tampa is set to premiere on the 15th of December on Netflix. And the show will feature 8 episodes of 45 minutes each.

In another post, Colony said that she was happy because she did not have to “keep this a secret anymore”. She was talking about her part in Selling Tampa, of course.

How Much Is Colony Reeves Net Worth?

Colony Reeves’s current net worth, i.e. as of 2021, was not more than $600 thousand. It might rise significantly now that she has become a reality TV star. Still, for someone who does not come from fortune, to make $600 thousand at the age of 30 is significant. So, how did Colony earn that much money?

As we have already mentioned, Colony is a realtor for Allure Realty LLC in Tampa. In the Florida realty industry, Colony is known as a very dependable real estate agent. Her specialty as a real estate agent lies in her professionalism, her incredible communication skills, and her incomparable local expertise.

As a realtor, Colony works with buys, sellers, and investors, thus creating an intricate network of people. And that helps her to match perfect sellers with perfect buyers and make everybody happy. Happiness is Colony’s goal. Colony maintains that her life’s mission is “to change lives through Real Estate”.

She has a few properties in her listings that cost from $275 thousand to $450 thousand. Usually, she deals with properties of a price range of $90 thousand to $650 thousand.

Before joining Allure Realty in October 2019, Colony used to work for Century 21 Beggins Enterprises for almost two years. And before that, she studied at the University of South Florida. She went to Tampa Catholic High School.
