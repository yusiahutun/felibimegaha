Taylor Swift Repeat-Wears This Do-It-All Spring Staple

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  414 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

All eyes are on Taylor Swift since the release of her double album, The Tortured Poets Department. Celebrating her work, Swift took to YouTube Shorts to post a recap full of ’Tayvis’ clips, behind the scenes moments, and leisure activities like baking and crafting. Hidden within the stitched video is a spring staple Swift wore not just once, but twice.

The artist styled both a white and black tank top on two separate occasions while receiving a kiss from Travis Kelce and playing pickleball. The unfussy shirt included cap sleeves and a square neckline, which flattered the décolletage, highlighted the neck, and complimented the shoulders. TL;DR, the clean lines and unique silhouette made an otherwise basic top sexy and refined.    

8 Square-Neck Tank Tops

Swift often reaches for timeless pieces bordering on minimalist designs, so it makes sense that she opted for such a classic separate in two contrasting colors. Similar staple tanks have nuzzled themselves into the palm of fashion, as well, with Selena Gomez, Kylie Jenner, and Hailey Bieber leading the charge—so the singer is definitely on to something.

Recreating Swift’s uncomplicated ensemble doesn’t have to set you back, either. Amazon offers a spot-on lookalike for $19, which boasts double-lined material and a “very flattering” fit that includes the “perfect length.” The $38 Free People Hummingbird Tank is another great pick including delicate ribbed cotton and a visible back seam. For something more elevated, consider the fitted Reformation Keegan Knit Top or stretchy AllSaints Tamir Rib Crop Tank; both include luxe fabrics, elegant cuts, and are available in black and white.

Swift-inspired square-neck tanks can be dressed up or down, too. Pair either hue with straight-leg jeans and Adidas Sambas, or dad shorts and Birkenstocks for a laid-back look. Alternatively, wear the chic tops with pleated maxi skirts and kitten heels, or blazers and leather pants. The boundless styling opportunities make cap-sleeve shirts an uncomplicated closet must-have that everyone needs at least one of. 

It’s a breath of fresh air when a celebrity—especially one as big as Miss Swift—wears something that’s actually practical and affordable. So, keep scrolling to find your new go-to spring top, including an $8 Amazon find.
