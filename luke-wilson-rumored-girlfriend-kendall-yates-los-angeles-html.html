In the eight-episode series, he will star alongside Ray Romano, Lisa Kudrow, Linda Cardellini, Teyonah Parris, Abbi Jacobson and Poppy Liu.

According to the official synopsis shared by Netflix, the story follows 'three very different families vying to buy the very same 1920s Spanish-style villa that they think will solve all their problems'. 

However, the plot takes a turn when the sellers discover that sometimes 'the home of your dreams can be a total nightmare'. 

In the show, Wilson will portray JD Campbell, a depressed, out-of-work soap opera star desperate to land his next job, who's looking to downsize from his modern McMansion while still maintaining both his lavish lifestyle and his marriage to a demanding wife. 

A release date for the series has not been announced.
