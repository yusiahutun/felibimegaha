Country music's finest brought glamour to the CMT Awards

    Country music's wildest party arrived in Texas on Sunday and some of the biggest names in the game made their way from Nashville, TN, to Austin for the 2023 CMT Music Awards.

    Hosted by Kelsea Ballerini and Kane Brown, the ceremony was one of the best yet. Alongside one-of-a-kind performances and world-class tributes, there was also some jaw-dropping fashion that graced the red carpet at the Moody Center.

    Carrie Underwood – who has undergone a stunning transformation – Carly Pearce, LeAnn Rimes, Gwen Stefani – who made her CMTs stage debut – and so many more rocked their finest ensembles, and you can see them all here.

    From leg-lengthening mini dresses to elegant floor-length designs in rainbow-spanning hues, the CMT Music Awards red carpet did not disappoint – see the best-dressed stars below…

    Carrie Underwood

    Carrie Underwood was almost upstaged by her incredible legs in her silver sequinned Dolce & Gabbana two-piece which she accessorized with Oliver Blandi jewelry. Her all-silver look sparkled on the red carpet, and she let her outfit take center stage by pulling her hair back into a low ponytail. 

    Lainey Wilson

    Lainey Wilson looked gorgeous in her red and blue flared jumpsuit that featured a plunging neckline. She added a studded belt for some edge and rocked her usual wide-brimmed hat, accessorizing with a Simitri clutch and ornate jewelry.  

    Shania Twain

    Shania Twain flashed a hint of leg in her sheer, red and black dress that also featured cut-out detailing on her waist and a subtle train. Her risque outfit even matched her red hair.

    Gwen Stefani

    Gwen Stefani brought her signature style to Texas. The singer mixed business with pleasure in Valentino, wearing fur-trimmed, knee-high boots, fishnet tights, a gold, sequinned mini skirt, a white shirt and tie, and a structured blazer. 

    LeAnn Rimes

    LeAnn Rimes looked stunning in her sheer, silver dress with applique detailing and a cheeky thigh split.

    Carly Pearce

     

    Carly Pearce opted for a classic LBD but with a twist. The skirt boasted a thigh-high slit on each side and the top featured a cut-out underneath the bust and silver embellishment across the neckline.

     Kelsea Ballerini

    Co-host Kelsea Ballerini looked gorgeous in her Dolce & Gabbana dress, David Yurman and Melinda Maria jewelry, and Jimmy Choo shoes.

    Madeline Edwards

    Madeline Edwards turned heads in her pink bodycon latex dress that flashed a hint of leg. 

     Dixie D'Amelio 

     Dixie D'Amelio stunned in her dreamy metallic ensemble that boasted pleated detailing and a wraparound waist. 

     Megan Thee Stallion

    Megan Thee Stallion rocked a figure-hugging custom Defïence dress in a standout royal blue hue, flashing a slither of skin thanks to cut-out detailing by her hip.

     Priscilla Block

    Priscilla Block looked beautiful in her curve-hugging denim skirt and matching cropped jacket that featured silver embellishments, a circle train, and a low-cut neckline. 

     Ashley McBryde

    Ashley McBryde stood out in white, showcasing several of her tattoos in this figure-hugging jumpsuit.

     Madison Bailey

    star Madison Bailey shone in her flirty yellow dress that boasted a pleated skirt, side split, and a sweetheart neckline.

     Alanis Morissette

    Alanis Morissette kept it classic in black, opting for a velvet pencil skirt, and a blouse that featured sheer sleeves.
