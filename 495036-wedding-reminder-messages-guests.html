50+ best wedding reminder messages for your guests in 2024

 Posted on March 15, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1655 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Weddings are beautiful, but they can also be overwhelming. Every couple that has had a wedding or is planning to have one in future will tell you that weddings require proper planning and management. A couple has to think of the meals and drinks that will be served, the venue, the wedding theme, the guests and much more. This is where wedding planners come in handy. Things like invitation cards and wedding reminder messages become easy to deal with when you have someone helping you.

How do you share wedding reminders for friends without appearing pushy or mean? You sent invitation cards. But what if you are still unsure of the headcount? Send gentle reminders. A reminder can be in the form of an email, a phone call, a text message or group messages on platforms like WhatsApp.

How do you write a wedding reminder message without making the recipient feel guilty? Start with cordial greetings and state your message. When sending reminders, make them short and to the point.

Wedding reminder for friends and family

People get busy and sometimes forget about events they have been invited to. Wedding reminders for guests are not to force one to attend the wedding or put the guest on the spot. Instead, they are to seek a response as some guests may not have seen the first invite.

Some guests also fail to respond on time, which can inconvenience everyone during the complete planning. Therefore, the language used in wedding reminders should be warm and gentle, as in the original invitation cards.

  • Hello dear friend, I'm asking if you will be available for our wedding. God bless.
  • Hi sister! We are cordially inviting you to our big day. Please share with me if you will make it.
  • How have you been? I hope life has been kind to you the way it has been to us. We are inviting you to our wedding.
  • Hello pal, I haven't heard from you since the last message. We are kindly inviting you to our special day on the dates shared.
  • Good morning lovelies! This message is a gentle reminder for all to RSVP for the wedding if they have not. Have a fruitful day, all.
  • Hello, best friend! I would love to have you witness our marriage. Your presence in the ceremony will mean everything to us.
  • Hello beautiful people. We need to share the total numbers with our caterer, so please side-chat me if you will be coming for the wedding. Love you!
  • Hi all! Just checking to see how many are up for reservation on my upcoming wedding. Thank you.
  • Hi, don’t forget about our wedding next week at our community church. Thank you.
  • I appreciate everyone for being with us during the planning. I am checking in to see how many of you will be available for the big day. Thanks.

WhatsApp wedding reminder messages

Whatsapp is among the most used messaging platforms online today. The mobile application features make it easy to convey a message that will reach the intended audience at once. Wedding reminder text message

Sending wedding invites is not as simple as it may appear. It is fun but not easy, as you have to individually select and send the cards to everyone you want to share your big day with. Therefore, wedding invites are best sent early so guests can prepare themselves.

  • Hi pals. In the spirit of having everyone around, we ask that you help us with the meal plan by adding your favourites.
  • Hello! Another warm reminder of our wedding. Thanks so much, and we hope to see you soon!
  • We don't mean to be pushy, but we ask that all of you confirm your names for our upcoming wedding.
  • Hello, my family! If you had not RSVP'd for the wedding via email, you can call me on my cell. Thank you.
