They regularly welcome actress Kate Hudson and her brother Oliver, from Goldie's previous marriage, Wyatt Russell, their hockey player son, and Kurt's son Boston from a previous marriage. 

With a full beard, Kurt appears to be enjoying quite the break from his filming commitments.
