Exclusive: Leona Lewis talks making her Broadway debut in Cats

 Posted on October 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  511 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

    Leona Lewis has a jam-packed schedule for the next few months. Not only is she a brand ambassador for beauty giants KISS, but the singer recently revealed she has landed the role of Grizabella in Broadway's revival of hit musical Cats. With just a few weeks to go before the show's previews kick off, the singer has been hard at work getting ready to make her Broadway debut.

    "I've had a fitting of the costume, we haven't got into make-up yet," she told HELLO! Online exclusively. "That will be later down the line. There's sketches and drawings of what Grizabella looks like, so I'm looking forward to really, really getting into character."

    Leona Lewis shared her beauty secrets exclusively with HELLO! Online

    No stranger to performing on stage in front of huge audiences, Leona admitted that although she's not feeling too nervous currently, she thinks it may be a different story on opening night.

    "I'm sure I will be nervous," she confessed. "Right now we're in rehearsals so it's just easing our way into it. But for sure, on the big night, it will be nerve-wracking. I get nervous before every performance."

    When she's not busy rehearsing, the hitmaker has been giving her legions of Instagram followers serious beauty envy, as she shows off her gorgeous make-up looks. Admitting that at the moment she's "loving rocking a red lip and a red nail", Leona revealed that she tries to follow a key beauty rule she has picked up over the years.
