Madden has quickly become the BBC’s new hunk, filling the boots left by The Night Manager’s Tom Hiddleston and Poldark’s Aidan Turner.

Asides from the steamy trysts, sinister themes continued throughout Sunday's show as the Home Secretary suffered another assassination attempt.

Budd crawled through debris to reach the minister, whose charred body appears lifeless.
