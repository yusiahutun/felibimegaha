Looking good and being a stylish fashionista simply comes easy to some people as Sophia Bush showed on Sunday afternoon.

However, the One Tree Hill star was almost left red-faced when her very chic white dress almost showed a little too much.

The 30-year-old looked majestic in her cute white dress but risked revealing her side boob as she and a friend left the nail salon in West Hollywood.

Spilling out: Sophia Bush very nearly showed her side boob as she wore a stylish dress to the nail salon on Sunday afternoon, in West Hollywood

Sophia, who did not appear to be wearing a bra, was very nearly caught short as she returned her car and the stylish dress gaped open.

It wasn't just her side that she was putting on show as she also had plenty of leg out as she teamed the sleeveless dress with a pair of black strappy sandals.

One thing that the Partners actor did keep hidden, however, were her eyes as she sported a pair of sunglasses with her long brunette locks blowing back in the gentle breeze.

Wear it well: Sophia looked superb as she wore a short white dress but the frock was not without its risks

Easy does it: Sophia made the routine trip to the nail salon that little bit more stylish in her cool getup

She accessorised the outfit nicely with a dainty colourful necklace made up of several small balls and she also carried a pink handbag with palm trees on.

With her newly painted blue nails looking shiny and new, Sophia jumped into her car and drove off.

Having directed a few episodes of One Tree Hill, and starred in numerous movie projects Sophia knows a thing or two about good movies.

The pretty brunette saw The Master over the weekend and clearly rated the film very highly as she told her near 600,000 followers about it.

She tweeted: 'If you don't go see The Master, there is officially no chance we'll ever been friends. What. A. Movie! Brilliant performances. #theMaster'.

Lazy days: Sophia was joined by a girl friend as she showed off her latest manicure of shiny metallic blue nails
