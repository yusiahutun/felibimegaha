Meaning of 120 by Bad Bunny

 Posted on February 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  406 words  |  Larita Shotwell

In Bad Bunny's song titled '120', the overall theme revolves around desire, temptation, and the intensity of physical attraction. The song explores the passionate and impulsive nature of relationships, as well as the risks and excitement that come with giving in to one's desires.One standout lyric in the song is "Por ti me monto en el carro y meto 120" (For you, I get in the car and go 120). This lyric not only emphasizes the singer's willingness to go above and beyond for their love interest but also symbolizes the desire to accelerate the relationship to unimaginable speeds. The theme here is the exhilaration of pushing boundaries and taking risks in pursuit of love.Another intriguing lyric is "Me está' tentando, tú ere' una serpiente" (You're tempting me, you're a snake). This line symbolizes the seductive nature of the love interest, who entices the singer with their allure and charm. The theme here is the captivating and somewhat dangerous allure of forbidden or intense attractions.The lyric "Bad Bunny en una pista e' un Bugatti en un carril" (Bad Bunny on a track is like a Bugatti on a lane) represents the artist's confidence and self-assuredness in his craft. It highlights the importance of embracing one's talents and uniqueness, emphasizing the theme of self-confidence and success."Tú cumple' en octubre, pero yo te quiero abril" (Your birthday's in October, but I want you in April) suggests the singer's impatience and eagerness to have their love interest in their life as soon as possible. This lyric explores the theme of the overwhelming desire to hasten the progression of a relationship and the excitement that comes with the anticipation of being together.The mention of "la casa en P.R. o la de Beverly Hills" (the house in P.R. or Beverly Hills) demonstrates the notion of escapism and creating an idealized fantasy world with one's partner. It signifies the desire for an extravagant and luxurious lifestyle, giving expression to the theme of longing for a perfect and glamorous love story.Overall, '120' by Bad Bunny delves into the powerful emotions and desires that fuel passionate relationships. The song explores themes such as risk-taking, temptation, impatience, confidence, and the aspirations for an extraordinary love story. It captures the intense and all-consuming nature of attraction and the lengths people are willing to go to fulfill their desires. Through its lyrics, the song authentically portrays the complex emotions and experiences associated with love and passion.
