15 Celebrity Couples With Major Height Differences

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  291 words  |  Larita Shotwell

There’s nothing more disorienting than realizing the actor who looked so large in stature on your television screen is actually much smaller than you thought. The same concept applies to these famous celebrity couples who have surprisingly shocking height differences. With one partner who’s super tall and the other who can barely reach his or her shoulder, these couples would make you do a double take if you ever spotted them on the street!

Isla Fisher, who starred in Wedding Crashers and Confessions of a Shopoholic, for example, is a red-headed Hollywood sweetheart we can’t get enough of. Coming in at only 5-foot-3, she is towered over by her 6-foot-3 husband, comedian and actor Sacha Baron Cohen. And they’re certainly not the only A-list stars who just happened to couple up with someone of a totally different height.

Country superstar Blake Shelton and wife, Grammy-winner Gwen Stefani also find themselves among these celebrities with major height differences. With Blake at 6-foot-5 and Gwen at 5-foot-6, there’s a big difference in stature between these two love birds.
