15 classic 1930s mens hairstyles

 Posted on May 6, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1047 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Are you wondering why we refer the 1930s hairstyles as classic yet its decades ago? It seems to be old fashioned, but in real sense, most of the popular hairstyles were developed in this era. And that is the moment when people began imitating celebrities with their classic 1930s mens hairstyles, just as how people do it now.

These styles confirm that modern hairstyles and cuts are just a development of the 1930s haircuts and the mid 30s mens hairstyles. The cuts were well done that if you contrast with the modern haircuts and styles, you will see the uniqueness in them.

The 1930s mens hairstyles and haircuts

These are some of the 1930s men's hairstyles and haircuts that include the undercuts, short and curly hairstyles. Take a look and pick one to try during your next visit to the barber's shop.

1. The Fred Astaire style

The hair layout looks excellent and unique. The hair was gelled then combed in style.

The upper part and the right side was combed backwards. Then the left part was combed downwards forming a line on the upper left side of the head. This style also made Fred to good in movies like Top Hat and Funny Face.

2. Elegant men's hairstyle

When invited to a wedding party, the first thing that runs into your mind is the dressing and your hair. Same way in the 1930s people used to dress well and make their hair look good. This style was the most popular men's hairstyle because it looked good and decent. In this style, the hair was combed backwards.

3. Tom Hanks haircut

If you love curly hair, then take a look at Tom Hanks of The Green Mile movie hairstyle. Its a perfect example of how men in the 1930s with curled hair looked. The hair is styled in a perfect way to match the vintage style. If you see curly hair styled, remember it is just a change from this early years hairstyle.

4. Tom Hardy classic haircut

Tom Hardy actor of the Lawless movie haircut is the most popular hairstyle even in the present days. Also, his undercut is just the same as the present-day undercut.

This style was achieved by having an undercut, and then the remaining hair is gelled to hold it in position, giving out a perfect classic haircut.

5. Christoph Waltz haircut

Waltz, who is an Academy Award-winning actor, portrayed the 1930s men's hairstyles. The side parts, upper, and a combination of chevron moustache gives a real 30s look. The moustache is not popular in the present days.

6. Matthew Goode slick hair

The 2018 adaptation of the Brideshead Revisited, Matthew Goode portrays the second Charles Ryder, where he portrays a better 30s haircuts. The short and black hair is gelled, then the upper hair is slicked back with a slight taper fade on the sides.

The famous ballet dancer who was chosen to play the Prince on The Orient Express displays another mid 30s men's hairstyle. The slick back hair is the 30s way of styling the hair. The way he combs the hair makes him look unique in his own right.

8. Jesse Eisenberg hair

When the actor starred in the Woody Allen's Café Society, he displays a perfect 1930s hairstyle. The hair is well-laid, forming some finger waves. This is one of the coolest 1930s hairstyles.

9. Clark Gable style

Clark Gable was an actor during the shooting of Manhattan Melodrama in the year 1934. He was one of the most famous actors during his time that made people copy his hair styling.

Just as most of the 30s options, it was well-laid and then combed backwards.

10. Errol Flynn Fringe style

The actor on the set of The Private Lives of Elizabeth and Essex in 1939. Errol was a star of the decade. His cut was also on its level. The hair which was styled at different levels, coiling the upper part and styling the side parts. This made his style classic.

11. James Roosevelt style

James Roosevelt also had a hairstyle that was copied by people during his time. Just as most of the 30s hairstyles, his hair was combed back but some fell on the face. The style looked classic.

12. 1930s curly men's hairstyle

The curly hairstyle also rocked during the 30s. This style featured an undercut and gelled upper part. This styling is not very different from the present undercut and curls.

13. Indiana Jones style

This option can be described as a perfect 1930s men's hairstyles. His style was done by gelling the hair to put it in place, and the left side part was combed downwards then the remaining hair to the right. This left a gap on the upper left side but not forming a line like most of other mid 30s men's hairstyles.

14. Burt Lancaster style

Another perfect curled 1930s men's hairstyles. Burt Lancaster had extra coiled hair that looked classy. The sides are more extended, combed upwards then coiled well to be of the same size with the upper part hair. This hairstyle portrayed a young aged man option.

15. 1930s Bussiness men's style

Just as for the present days where people in business wear classy dressing codes with decent hair, the same happened in the 1930s. This is an example of a business men's hairstyle.

All it takes is a classic cut

The above list contains styles of cuts that men rocked back at the start of the previous century. Now you can confirm that most of the modern options are just a twist of the mid 30s styles. Also, you can try one of them and still look classy. If you are thinking of trying one of the 1930s mens hairstyles, then you should be prepared with a lot of gel to maintain the hair.
