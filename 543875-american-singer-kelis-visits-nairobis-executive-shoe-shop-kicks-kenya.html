American Singer Kelis Rodgers Visits Nairobi's Famous Shoe Shop after Meeting Sakaja

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  3 minutes  |  598 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon
  • US singer Kelis Rodgers had a flurry of special moments in Kenya, including hanging out with Governor Johnson Sakaja and spending time at Kicks Kenya, a local shoe shop
  • The R&B singer was thoroughly impressed by the sneakers available at Kicks Kenya and she even promoted them
  • Kicks Kenya's founder opened up about their journey in the business and revealed some celebrities they have worked with

US singer and songwriter Kelis Rodgers had a memorable time in Kenya.

While in Kenya, she visited a renowned shoe shop, Kicks Kenya, in Nairobi.

What's Kicks Kenya?

Kicks Kenya has become the ultimate destination for sneaker enthusiasts who appreciate style and quality.

The R&B star's impromptu visit to the shop brought immense happiness to the store's owner.

TUKO.co.ke had the opportunity to speak with the shop's founder and this is what she had to say about Kelis's visit.

“Kelis visited just like any other customer,” shared a Kicks Kenya representative. “Her passion for sneakers led her straight to the best store in Kenya.”

According to their revelations, Kick Kenya has catered to a host of celebrities, including Fally Ipupa, Kobbysalm, Arrowbwoy, Nadia Mukami, Kwambox, Nviiri, Bensoul, and Timeless Noel.

TUKO.co.ke also took the opportunity to ask a few questions, and the answers shed light on the shop’s continued success.

1. Why did you start the business?

The main vision and mission of Kicks Kenya is cemented into music, streetwear, and sneaker culture. Kicks Kenya stands as a beacon of excellence in the realm of high-end sneaker retail, offering a curated selection of sneakers that not only meet but transcend the expectations of discerning enthusiasts. Rooted in a deep appreciation for the sneaker culture, Kicks Kenya has not just carved a niche for itself but has become the undisputed pinnacle of sneaker retail in Kenya.

2. What's your secret?

What sets us apart is not just the extensive inventory of premium sneakers, but the unwavering commitment to principles of trust and customer satisfaction. In an industry where authenticity and quality are paramount, Kicks Kenya has established itself as a trusted destination, where every pair of sneakers tells a story and meets the highest standards of craftsmanship.

3. What keeps you motivated?

The past year has witnessed an extraordinary trajectory for Kicks Kenya, marked by substantial growth that has catapulted it to the zenith of retail excellence in Kenya. This remarkable ascent is a testament to the store's keen understanding of the market, a finely tuned sense of the pulse of sneaker culture, and an unyielding dedication to delivering unparalleled experiences to its customers.
