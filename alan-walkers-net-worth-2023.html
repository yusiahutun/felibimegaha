Career

DJ and Music Producer

Marshmello is a hugely talented DJ and music producer with numerous certified multi-platinum hits that appeared in the Billboard Hot 100 Top 300.

Managed by Moe Shalizi, Marshmello’s musical style includes groove-oriented, synth, and bass-heavy electronic dance music.

Early Career

Starting in 2015, Marshmello built his reputation off the back of popular song remixes which he released online, building a dedicated fanbase.

Having established himself in the electronic music scene, he released his debut album, “Joytime,” showcasing his original music and production skills.

The album was a defining moment in his career as it established Marshmello as a highly skilled musician with a unique, groove-oriented style.

‘Keep it Mello” was the album’s lead single, featuring a collaboration with Mexican rapper, Omar Linx who contributed to the song’s energetic sound.

A game-changing moment for Marshmello occurred with the release of his Platinum-certified hit, “Alone,” which achieved success in the USA and Canada.

Live Performances

A key component of Marshmello’s success was his appearance at live events, including various music festivals which helped him connect with his audience.

His appearances at events like Miami Music Week, HARD Day of the Dead festival in Pomona, and New York’s Pier 94 massively expanded his reach.

It wasn’t long before established artists like Skrillex took notice of his phenomenal work, re-posting his song “Find Me” on SoundCloud and endorsing his work.

With his increasing visibility worldwide, Marshmello maintained his hidden identity, appearing in public with a custom white helmet resembling a marshmallow.

With his true identity remaining a closely guarded secret, he achieved notable success on Billboard’s charts, peaking at number five on the Dance/Electronic Songs chart.

“Joytime” hit #14 on the US Heatseeker Albums chart, and forty-one on the Independent Albums chart, making a huge impact on the electronic music scene.

A Genre-Defining Sound

Marshmello’s characteristic sound with its energetic and bass-heavy sound accompanied by memorable melodies, set a precedent for his future music productions.

The combination of his captivating music and enigmatic persona created a real sense of intrigue and excitement among an ever-increasing number of fans.

This laid the foundation for his continued success in the music industry, resulting in a host of highly successful collaborations with other mainstream musicians.

His hookup with American R&B singer, Khalid, resulted in the hit single “Silence,” which was released in August 2017 through RCA Records.

The song achieved both platinum and multi-platinum certifications in numerous countries, solidifying Marshmello’s status as a top-tier electronic music producer.

Collaboration Successes

His next move was to team up with global pop icon, Selena Gomez, late in 2017, releasing the chart-topping single “Wolves.”

The single achieved huge success across the world, becoming one of his most commercially successful songs and catapulting him into the mainstream music scene.

He then followed this success with a successful collaboration hit, “Friends,” a catchy, upbeat track with relatable lyrics featuring British singer, Anne-Marie.

Worldwide Tours

Marshmello embarked on the “Ritual Tour,” performing in various countries, including the United States, China, South Korea, India, and Paraguay.

His global tour allowed him to connect more intimately with diverse audiences across cultures and languages, solidifying his international fan base.

Record Label Owner

Building on his phenomenal worldwide success, Marshmello founded a record label, “Joytime Collective,” allowing him to release his own music.

His label also gave him access to emerging artists, whom he could support and promote, contributing to the growth of the entire electronic music community.

Marshmello continued to collaborate with electronic music producers, releasing singles like “Chasing Colors” with Ookay and Slushii’s, “Twinbow.”

“Moving On” was his next emotional single, with the song’s music video receiving millions of views and resonating with fans across the globe.

He’s announced upcoming collaborations with American hip-hop artist Blackbear and pop star Demi Lovato, generating huge excitement among fans.

Marshmello released “Love U” as a free single in appreciation of his fans, described as a “gritty dance-pop single with a pounding bass line.”

Philanthropist 

Beyond the electrifying beats and catchy melodies, Marshmello uses his influence and resources to make a positive impact on various charitable causes.

A notable contribution was teaming up with Fortnite player Tyler “Ninja” Blevins to participate in Epic Games’ E3 Celebrity Pro Am charity tournament.

Marshmello donated half his $1 million winnings to KIND (Kids in Need of Defense), an organization providing legal counsel to refugees and immigrant children.

He also supports animal welfare causes, with his single “Happier” accompanied by a pet-dog-themed music video, promoting the #FindYourFido campaign.
