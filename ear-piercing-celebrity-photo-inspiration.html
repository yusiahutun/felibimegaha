Ear piercing inspiration: from Florence Pugh to Kaia Gerber

 Posted on February 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  466 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Let it be known, curating a seriously stylish ear-scape (it's a thing, promise) requires great attention to detail. Surely adding one more off-the-cuff piercing couldn't possibly hurt? Actually, it really, really could. Step away from the piercing parlour, until further notice.

MORE: 7 stylish chunky rings to boost your jewellery game

When it comes to designing your perfect piercing situ, balance is key. Over the years, it is well possible that you may have amassed more piercings than you care to remember, and so, a little thoughtful curation might be in order.

And what better way to get those creative juices flowing than by checking out some of our current favourite celebrity ear-scapes for inspiration.

Florence Pugh

 

Leading lady Florence Pugh knows what's up when it comes to curating the perfect earring collection. Recently on the Venice Film Festival red carpet, she showed off her super strong jewellery game by nodding to the silver flecks on her dazzling gown via a celestial cluster piece and a dainty lightning bolt.

Kaia Gerber

Kaia Gerber's snake bite is everything, and more. Featuring two studs alongside one another – one rounded pearlescent piece and the other a solar-style emblem – the supermodel's ear-scape has got us adding a trip to the piercing parlour to our to-do list.

Rihanna

 

The singer-cum-beauty-mogul has not only got us lusting after her killer Fenty lip glosses, now we want to recreate her utterly stylish piercing collection. We adore the slightly gothic glamour of Rihanna's pendant silver crucifixes alongside her chic tragus tri-stud.

 READ: How to wear ear cuffs: nail one of the hottest celebrity jewellery trends

Kylie Jenner

 

Never underestimate the power of a sole, thick helix hoop, and we thank Kylie Jenner for leading the way. Once in a blue moon, less actually is more – yeah, we were shocked too. A sleek bun invites the eye to admire the lone earring, and the coolness of the silver pairs harmoniously with her shimmery azure dress.

Dove Cameron

 

Whimsigoth poster girl Dove Cameron is all about blending glossy blacks and shiny silvers, and we're very much into it. Linking her conch and lobe piercings via a flowing silver chain is such an inspired idea…and now we want one too.

Ashley Graham

 

Model Ashley Graham slicked her hair back to allow her earring situation to do the talking, and, rest assured, it did not disappoint. With a flower in her conch, rectangular gems in her helixes and an encrusted spiral in her lobe, she proved that organised chaos is a perfectly valid vibe choice.
