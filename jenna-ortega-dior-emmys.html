The making of Jenna Ortega's beautiful Dior gown for the Emmys

 Posted on July 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  260 words  |  Martina Birk

They say good things come to those who wait, and Jenna Ortega's red-carpet look for the delayed 2023 Emmys was the perfect example of that. The Wednesday star was one of our best-dressed of the night – joining the likes of Issa Rae in feathered Pamella Roland and Ayo Edebiri in black Louis Vuitton leather – in her beautifully intricate Dior gown, which was covered in floral detail.

Ortega opted for a voluminous embroidered strapless gown by Dior for the evening, crafted from beautiful tulle and silver, green and pale pink embroidery that shimmered beautifully on the red carpet. As expected for something so intricate, a lot of work went into the design. This included 700 hours of embroidery and 400 hours of work to assemble.

Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

Courtesy of Dior

To match the glimmering sequins of her dress, she carried on the theme with embellished heels and a Gem Dior ring, a Dior Joaillerie bracelet and a white gold and diamond necklace (which works perfectly with the neckline of her gown), as well as Rose Dior Couture earrings.

Courtesy of Dior

Courtesy of Dior

Beauty-wise, Ortega's make-up artist Melanie Inglessis used all Dior Beauty products, including Rouge Dior in #976 Daisy Plum.

Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

The actress was nominated at last night's Emmys for her performance in Tim Burton's Wednesday on Netflix, while she also earned a Golden Globe nomination for the Addams Family reboot.
