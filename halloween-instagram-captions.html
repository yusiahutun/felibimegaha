158 Best Halloween Instagram Captions 2023

 Posted on February 27, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1335 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Ghosts and goblins and ghouls, oh my! Whether your Halloween costume is scary, funny, or cute, you need to show it off (and make all the other witches jealous). The best way to do that? On Instagram, of course! Bonus points if you have some clever Halloween captions up your sleeve for each and every post.

It doesn't matter if it's a DIY costume you spent hours crafting or a last-minute Halloween costume you bought online at 50 percent off—you'll want to document your spook-tacular 'fit for Halloween 2023. We suggest posing next to your perfectly painted (or carved) pumpkin with a caramel apple in hand or surrounded by haunting decorations. And don't forget the group and couple pics with your best ghouls and/or your boo.

Now, you may have used all your creative juices on your costume and can't think of something catchy to say. Don't worry. We've done the work for you and have gathered 153 of the best Halloween Instagram captions. From short and sweet phrases to classic scary movie quotes to Halloween jokes, these captions are fang-tastic. Now, just pick your most bewitching photo, choose a caption from this list, and watch the likes roll in! If you're still on a witch hunt for more Halloween captions, check out these Halloween puns too.
