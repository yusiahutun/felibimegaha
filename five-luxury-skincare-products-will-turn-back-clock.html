Five luxury skincare products that will turn back the clock

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  285 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The latest luxury skincare that will help turn back the clock on your skin’s appearance:

Cle de Peau

Le Sérum, the brand’s new addition to what it now calls The Radiance Trio (along with the Intensive Eye Contour Cream and The Cream), is fortified with its Illuminating Complex EX. The formula contains platinum colden silk essence, Japanese pearl and theanine to increase your skin’s clarity and luminosity. HK$2,300

La Prairie

The new Skin Caviar Absolute Filler features the brand’s Caviar Absolute formula, which contains highly concentrated caviar oil and proteins. The powerful formula stimulates collagen formation and is designed to firm and plump up the skin. HK$4,980

La Mer

The Genaissance de la Mer collection has two new additions this month, one of which is the Infused Lotion. The sleek, silky texture of the oil-in-water lotion is perfect for autumn, and the Crystal Miracle Broth in the formula firms the skin and lays a smooth, healthy foundation for serum application. HK$2,200

MDNA

It’s not often that we feature a skincare tool rather than the actual product, but the Magnetic Flow is not your everyday applicator. The sleek, silver instrument, designed to fit all the different contours of your face, is meant to be used with the brand’s Chrome Clay Mask. The applicator’s magnetic qualities lifts the mask thoroughly from the skin and stimulates the skin’s keratin layer. HK$1,300

Rodial

The millennial pink trend just keeps on going. The brand, best known for its exotically named products, is launching a new Rose Gold collection. In addition to a serum and a filler, the collection also has a moisturiser, which is infused with white rose cells, vitamin C and the brand’s 24 carat gold technology. HK$1,990
