Amanda Holden biography

The bubbly entertainer was born as Amanda Louise Holden in Portsmouth, Hampshire in the United Kingdom. Miss Holden was born to Frank Holden, her father, and Judith, her mother. Both are of British descent. The two raised their kids in Bishop Walthman in South Hampshire.

Education and early life

Amanda joined the Little Theatre Company when she was 9 years. She later joined Swanmore Secondary school. She was active in co-curricular and took part in theatre displays put together by her school.
