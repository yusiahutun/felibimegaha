Lorde has raised eyebrows by sharing very intimate photos of herself listening to music in the bath. 

The New Zealand singer, 27, took to Instagram on Wednesday with four images of herself posing nude and makeup free in a green marble bathtub surrounded by candles, epsom salts and lotions. 

Her hair soaking wet, Lorde listened to music in a pair of black headphones and sported an array of gold jewellery. 

The hitmaker also wore a solemn expression while strategically covering her modesty with her arms. 

'Full immersion,' she simply captioned the post.
