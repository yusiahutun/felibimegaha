Who is Joe Pesci?

Joseph Frank Pesci was born in Newark, New Jersey USA, on 9 February 1943, so his zodiac sign is Aquarius and he holds American nationality. He is an actor and a musician, best known for starring in movies about he mafia such as “Raging Bull” and “The Irishman” among numerous others.

Joe started appearing in theatre plays at the age of five; he attended Belleville High School, and after matriculation focused on acting and didn’t enroll at college.

While still attending high school, Joe started working as a barber, while he later became a guitar player, joining Joey Dee and the Starliters band. He made his debut film appearance in 1976 in “The Death Collector”, and three years later he was contacted by famous actor Robert De Niro and director Martin Scorsese who wanted him for the film “Raging Bull”. The movie became a huge hit, and Joe won a BAFTA Film Award for newcomer to leading film roles for his performance. In 1984, Robert De Niro contacted him once again to star alongside him in “Once Upon a Time in America”, which was a hit worldwide.

He went on to portray Leo Getz in each sequel of the comedy film “Lethal Weapon” which stars Mel Gibson, and then appeared in another hit movie – “Goodfellas” – alongside Robert De Niro, winning an Academy Award for best supporting actor for his performance. Joe could also have been seen portraying Harry Lyme in “Home Alone” and “Home Alone 2: Lost in New York”. Some of his latest roles have been in movies “Love Ranch” in 2010, “A Warrior’s Tail” in 2015, and “The Irishman” in 2019, which once again stars Martin Scorsese and Robert De Niro.

Joe married his first wife in 1964 and she gave birth to their daughter, but the two divorced before he met Claudia. He met Angie Everhart, an actress and model, in 2006 and the two got engaged in 2007, but split in 2008.
