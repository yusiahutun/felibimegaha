Logan Paul's extravagant 'proposal' didn't go as planned after 'rookie mistake'

 Posted on July 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  403 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Logan Paul is reportedly engaged to his girlfriend Nina Agdal, just weeks after they celebrated their one-year anniversary. The influencer turned WWE superstar, reportedly popped the question in Lake Como, Italy, on Sunday night.

The proposal is said to have taken place on the rooftop of a luxury hotel around the most profound lake in Italy, according to eyewitnesses. The pair have not yet gone public with the news, although Logan Paul recently posted an Instagram story of the loved-up couple kissing on a boat in the area.

Logan Paul reportedly ‘engaged’ to Nina Agdal after one year of dating

Logan Paul reportedly proposed to his girlfriend in their hotel in Lake Como, Italy, according to sources from the Daily Mail. It comes just after the pair celebrated their one-year anniversary, on May 17.

Less than a week after the reports, Logan announced his engagement via his Instagram, in a post that read: “Engaged to my best friend.”

Eyewitnesses told the publication that the two were ‘visibly overjoyed’ with Logan reportedly calling his celebrity brother Jake to share the good news afterward.

However, allegedly things weren’t all smooth sailing, as eyewitnesses also reported that the ring didn’t fit on Nina’s finger. Nonetheless, the couple were over the moon, sharing an ’emotional embrace.’

Who is Nina Agdal?

Nina Agdal is a 31-year-old Danish model, mostly known for her appearances in Sports Illustrated, including the cover for their 50th-anniversary special, in 2014.

At the time of writing, she has 1.7 million followers on Instagram, although has not yet posted about the reported engagement. Her last post was a selfie from three weeks ago, captioned: “Reading your mind but keeping it to myself.”

The model also reportedly dated Leonardo Dicaprio, between 2016-2017. She then started dating YouTube star Logan Paul in 2022, a year before they got ‘engaged.’

Logan Paul and Nina’s relationship leading up to the ‘engagement’

Paul and Agdal dating rumors began circulating in June 2022, when the couple were spotted looking cozy at the London restaurant, Novikov.

However, the two remained tight-lipped about their relationship for a while, until the WWE superstar confirmed their relationship in December, with an Instagram post of the two, captioned: “Lucky me.”

Cut to May 2023, and the pair celebrated their one-year anniversary, with Paul posting a slideshow of photos with his girlfriend, alongside the caption: “One very special year with my Danish queen.”
