She was making her very first appearance as a Loose Women panellist.

The 23-year-old stunner, who is dating grime star Stormzy, was sitting on the panel alongside Andrea McLean, Penny Lancaster and Janet Street-Porter when she spoke candidly about the shocking moment she realised she was in the midst of an orgy.

Aside from her stranger line of work, Maya - who ensured she looked sensational in a folksy dress with embroidery and a floor-sweeping hem - also touched on Internet safety and how she was 'Catfished' when she was just 14 in an online chat room.

She explained: 'I’ve had a couple of catfish experiences, firstly when I was about 14-years-old which was actually quite worrying, back then it wasn’t really spoken about – Catfish wasn’t such a popular thing, the show wasn’t out.

‘I was talking on MSN to someone I’m not sure how I met, maybe on other network like Bebo or Habbo Hotel or something, speaking to this guy for a while and messaging and everytime I’d ask him to go on webcam.
