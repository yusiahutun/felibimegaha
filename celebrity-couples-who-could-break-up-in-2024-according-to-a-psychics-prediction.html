Celebrity couples who could break up in 2024 according to a psychic's prediction

 Posted on May 23, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  722 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Exclusive: 2024 has just begun, and we hate to start on a downer, but a Celebrity Tidbit sat down with a psychic who told us which three major break ups among the stars he predicts for the year ahead.

The past 12 months was a huge year for celebrity break ups, and it looks like 2024 is set to be similar, celebrity psychic Adam Norton predicts. From a long-standing relationship to another who has barely got started, here’s what he’s told us on the unexpected potential heartbreak in Hollywood. Not only that, but one royal couple has unfortunately made the list of predictions.

Celebrity break up 2024 psychic predictions: Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel

The first shock prediction, Adam says, is music star Justin Timberlake and actress Jessica Biel. The celebrity couple have been married for 10 years, and together for 16, but Norton thinks it may be the end of the road for these two this year.

“When it comes to divorces, Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel are at the top of the pecking list,” he warns.

Their love story began at a party in 2007 where they “just connected”. In March 2011, the pair briefly broke up, but they found their way back together and got engaged in December of that year.

Channeling his ex-Britney Spears, it looks like the divorce is predicted to be a Toxic one. “I do see a separation ahead for them, and it’ll feel somewhat toxic too,” the celeb psychic believes.

The former NSYNC star hasn’t had the best of years, as Britney released her memoir The Woman In Me, making claims about her ex, including the breakup text he allegedly sent, leaving her in tears.

Although Justin has chosen to stay quiet about the allegations, it seemed like he may have referenced his ex last month during a Las Vegas concert. Before performing his hit, Cry Me A River, thought to be about Spears, he announced: “No disrespect”.

Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper

A much newer couple, Bradley Cooper and Gigi Hadid, sparked dating rumors in October 2023 when they were spotted together in New York City.

At first, it was claimed the two were just ‘having fun’ but it looks like things have become more serious as the Hollywood star has reportedly purchased a home near the Hadid’s family residence.

However, sources told US Weekly they were yet to ‘define’ their relationship, and it looks like there may be no point, according to Adam’s predictions, as he’s ‘certain’ they won’t last.

“Gigi Hadid is rumored to be dating Bradley Cooper, but this definitely won’t last, and I’m certain of that,” he told us.

“I’ll be VERY surprised if it goes past April / May time,” he continued, before telling us he sees a new love on the horizon.

He also predicts “Gigi going down a bit of a rabbit hole on a personal level,” saying there are a few bumps in the road ahead for her.

Prince Harry and Meghan Markle

One shocking (or not) prediction made by the celeb psychic was the downfall of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle‘s relationship.

The two met after being set up by friends in July 2016, and they quickly became serious, announcing their engagement just over a year later.

They tied the knot in a lavish royal wedding in May 2018, and although the two have remained strong, their relationship with the rest of the royals hasn’t, as they left the institution in 2020.

Now, it looks like Adam predicts their relationship with each other will come to an end this year. “Definitely seeing a separation ahead for Harry and Meghan, and I actually feel Meghan will return back to work, so her career is about to take off in a different direction,” he predicts.

The former Suits star gave up acting when she got engaged to Prince Harry, however their move to LA has people thinking she wants the Hollywood lifestyle back.

As well as onscreen work, Adam predicts Meghan will also be working behind the camera, but we can expect to see her on the big screen soon.

He also predicts the Duke will “miss the UK” and ‘regret’ his separation from the rest of the family.
