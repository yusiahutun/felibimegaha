Selena Gomez Carried the Glitziest Version of This Celeb-Loved Handbag

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  349 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Selena Gomez might be besties with Taylor Swift and own one of the best beauty brands, IMO,  but she’s just like us. The multi-hyphenate hard-launches relationships on social media, loves a good sweatshirt, and carries viral, affordable handbags.       

At the 81st Golden Globe Awards, Gomez wore a high-low Armani gown and red Christian Louboutin stilettos, but her outfit was completed by a widely popular under $150-accessory: the Jw Pei Gabbi handbag. Available on Amazon, the purse has won the approval of A-listers such as Megan Fox, Gigi Hadid, Hailey Bieber, Emily Ratajkowski, and more.   

Jw Pei Gabbi Crystal Handbag

Gomez opted for the purse in a ruby-red hue and an all-new glitzy rhinestone design. It’s an upgraded take on the brand’s iconic faux leather purse, while still encapsulating the details that made the OG so great, such as the cinched drop handle, dumpling-like construction, and interior pocket. Better yet, her exact bag is still available for $110 on the brand’s website with the code 24WS15.

The reasonably priced vegan handbag isn’t only up for grabs in one spicy red color, but it's also available in striking black, shiny silver, and lime green, allowing you to find your perfect match. Best of all, each of the three colors is available on Amazon, so thanks to quick Prime shipping, you can spend less time waiting for your delivery and more time wearing your fun, exciting bag. 

While embellishments can seem daunting, they don’t have to be. A good place to start is with a solid-colored outfit. Whether you opt for the red, black, green, or silver Jw Pei Gabbi crystal bag, it’ll look effortless against an undistracting canvas. The purse will become the focal point of your ensemble, instantly leveling it up no matter how hard you did or didn’t try. In other words, let your new bag do the talking.   

If the rhinestone Gabbi handbag follows the original’s popularity, then these styles are bound to go fast — especially with Gomez carrying the on-sale red colorway. Shop your choice hue before it sells out.
