1. Great Body Gym

Great Body Gym is located at the CBD at Interfina House along Ronald Ngala Street. Great body gym charges a monthly membership subscription is Ksh. 6,000.

The gym offers a vast array of services. At Great Body Gym, you can access Taekwondo, Taebo, boxing, cardio, Zumba, aerobics, steam, bodybuilding, karate, weight-lifting, and tummy trimming among other services.

The monthly subscription is Ksh.6,000, but the plan gets better when you subscribe for long-term engagement with the gym. For three months the subscription is Ksh. 16,000. For half-yearly the subscription is Ksh. 28,000 while for a full-year commitment you will pay Ksh. 50,000. These membership plans will help you save big on your routine exercise. For walk-in customers, the gym charges Ksh.500 daily.

Before subscribing to any of these packages, you will have to register with Ksh. 200. You can visit Great Body Gym website to know more about the services they offer, and also keep an eye open just in case they have best offers on membership.

2. Premier Fitness Centre

Premier Fitness Centre is located at Finance House along the Koinange Street within the CBD. Premier Fitness Centre is one of the good gym in Nairobi with basic membership as low as Ksh. 6,000 per month.

Premier Fitness Centre has a large space that can accommodate both corporate clients as well as team exercises. They major in boxing, but also offer other services as; yoga Pilates, indoor cycling, full-body workout, and cardio fitness.

For corporate clients, they charge Ksh. 44,500 per month. Their doors open at 6:00 am to 10:00 pm from Monday to Thursday. On Friday, they open at 6.00am and close at 11:00 pm.

On Saturdays they open at 7:30 am and close at 11:30 pm while on Sundays they open at 7:30 am and close at 9:30 pm. The flexibility in their opening hours allows them to customize training sessions according to their clients’ availability.

They also have spacious changing rooms that are clean and best fitted for highbrow clientele. Plus, they have fitness challenges that spur fitness lovers to achieve great workout milestones within a short time. These fitness challenges run periodically and are open to every member who likes fitness challenges. To know about this gym, visit this website.

3. Figure 8

Figure 8 gym is purely a women’s gym that is located along Tom Mboya Street. The basic membership is Ksh.6,000.

In figure 8, you can have a personal trainer to help you achieve your dream figure. Some of the services that you can access include; massage, personal training, spray tanning, and beds.

Others subscriptions include; Ksh.60,000 for a whole year, a one-time walk-in fee of Ksh.3000. Figure 8 is a good gyms in Nairobi CBD for women. For more information, visit their website.

4. Hotel Central Park Health Club

Hotel Central Park Health club is an exercise facility of the Hotel. The gym is modern and well-equipped. It is located on Sheikh Karume road next to Tuskys supermarket. If you are familiar with Nairobi Streets, this gym is located between Ronald Ngala Street and Luthuli Avenue.

Some of the services you can find in this gym include; full gym equipment, sauna, steam bath, spa, massage parlor, and beauty lounge.

Though they don’t show the charges for the gym on their website; it is located in downtown and hence affordable. You can get more information by walking into the hotel and inquiring about the prices, or you can contact the management through these contacts; Phone; (+254) 0714036666 / 0714036555, emails: millicent@hotelcentralpark.net, sales@hotelcentralpark.net. You can also visit their website to learn more.

5. The Saints Health Club

The Saints Health Club is located at All-Saints Cathedral, opposite Serena Hotel. This gym charges a minimum of Ksh.9,500 per month for individual members.

The gym has world-class fitness facilities and is set in a quiet neighborhood that makes the perfect sync of the body and mind during the exercise.

The Saints Health Club offers such services as; Steam and Sauna, cardio fitness, group exercise, fruit bar, rest lounge, strength training, and treatment rooms.

The Saints Health Club has different membership categories. Students can have a monthly membership of Ksh. 7,000. Couples will pay Ksh.16,000 per month while individuals (non-students) will pay Ksh.9,500 per month.

For yearly subscriptions, individuals pay Ksh.93,000, couples pay Ksh. 170,000, while corporate clients pay Ksh.83,000.

As most clients attest, the gym has some of the most advanced facilities. It also employs the best instructors to serve its high-end clients. It opens its doors from 5:30 am to 9:00 pm on weekdays. On Saturday operates from 7:00 am to 7:00 pm while on Sundays the doors open at 11:00 am and closes at 6:00 pm. For more information, visit their website.

Other subscriptions include; Ksh. 15,000 for 12 sessions and Ksh.7,500 for six sessions. This gym has world-class cardio machines, aerobic studio, spin studio, fitwall classes among the equipment that will help you keep fit.

They open their doors from 6:00 am every morning and close late in the night. Their long operational hours allows gym enthusiasts who are stuck in their workplaces to keep fit without jeopardizing their job.

7. Crossfit Kwetu Gym

This gym is located along the GiriGiri lane and has a host of qualified instructors who will help you achieve your fitness goals. You can begin your training sessions with a lower subscription of Ksh.7,500 per month.

Laurasam fitness gym is a top-class exercise setting that not only concentrate on your muscles and physique but also worries about your quality of life. They have diet guides to help you achieve your desired health priorities.
