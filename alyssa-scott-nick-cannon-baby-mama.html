Alyssa Scott Gives Birth To Baby Boy With Nick Cannon As The Father

Just a few days ago, in the last of June 2021, Alyssa Scott gave birth to her newborn baby boy and had shared the happy occasion via her Instagram account.

She took to Instagram on 3 July 2021 to reveal the exciting news as she shared three new photos, two of which showed her holding her newborn. She captioned it: “I will love you for eternity – 6.23.21.”

In her Instagram Stories, Alyssa revealed their baby’s name is Zen.

Although Alyssa first shared the news of her expectancy back in May, it wasn’t until Father’s Day that Nick was confirmed to be Zen’s dad. “Celebrating you today,” Alyssa wrote in an Instagram Story post featuring a maternity photo that included a shirtless Nick.

Nonetheless, each of her posts received a flood of congratulations from her Instagram followers.

Who Is Alyssa Scott? Age, Parents

Alyssa Scott was born on 12 October 1993 in Austin, Texas, USA.

According to newsunzip.com, her parents are Alan Scott and Lisa Marie Kalender. She was born into a well-settled business family and belongs to the Christian religion. She has a brother named Alexander ‘Alex’ Scott.

According to various sources, she completed her education at a local pirate school in her hometown. She then began working on her modeling career and now has become a well-known fashion model.

Alyssa is a model from Nick Cannon’s show Wild ‘n Out and has signed with several fashion and swimsuit brands over the years. She also owns an Instagram account, itsalyssaemm, which has amassed over 180k followers, where she frequently shares bikini and beachside photos. Her modeling photos go as far back as March 2015, when she first posted on her Instagram account.

The 27-year-old model reportedly stands at the height of 5 feet 8.5 inches (1.73 m). The blonde beauty is an animal lover and shares many photos of animals on her Instagram profile.
