Mrs Hinch fans 'can't stop sniffing' at winter rose and eucalyptus home range

 Posted on October 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  461 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Mrs Hinch has got fans rushing to shops as her sell-out home and fabric range is back with new scents just in time for winter.

The cleaning guru, whose real name is Sophie Hinchliffe, is known for sharing lifestyle hacks and home decoration tricks on social media and her previous laundry products launch was deemed a huge success.

This year she worked with P&G and Joy Of Clean to create some lovely winter scent for some of her best loved home care brands, including Fairy, Flash and Febreze. The cleaning goods will be available in Frosted Eucalyptus scent.

As for laundry products, Mrs Hinch's orginal Rose Wonderland scent, was given a new formula — a rose fragrance with white florals and fresh tones.

Fans were excited to find out about the new launch and said they can't wait to shop for the Bold laundry pods and washing liquid gel, Lenor fabric conditioner and scent booster, as well as Febreze fabric refreshener.

Mrs Hinch shared on Instagram with pictures of the cleaning products and the lovely flower arrangements her team has set up in her kitchen.

She said: "I can’t believe it’s been five years since I started working with P&G, and during that time I’ve been so lucky to work on so many brilliant limited edition ranges! This latest collection is amazing and I’m really excited for everyone to try it and bring it into their homes.

"My Winter Faves collection will see the return of the Frosted Eucalyptus scent, which received so much love, as well as the Frosted Rose Wonderland scent - inspired by my first Rose Wonderland collection. I hope you all love it as much as I do!"

One fan who visited his local supermarket commented: "Had a sniff of it all today when out shopping and girl! It’s now home with us ready to clean with and have fresh bedding on Sunday reset."

A second added: "Brought today in love with the smell absolutely love them all but the colour of these are so pretty!"

A third came back from Asda and shared: "This actually smells insane you know, I was in Asda like a weirdo sniffing the boosters so loud."

The limited edition range can be found in Sainsbury's, Tesco, The Range and other retailers. If you want to buy them in bulk, Amazon is a good option.

You can also get a Delphis supersized washing up liquid from Amazon to save money in the lead up to Christmas. Robert Dyas has a clearance promotion online for a wide range of goods including household and cleaning products.
