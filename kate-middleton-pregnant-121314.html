Is Kate Middleton expecting Baby No. 3?

The Duchess of Cambridge had fans thinking she may be pregnant after she was spotted holding her belly during a Cub Scout meeting in King’s Lynn, England on Wednesday, Dec. 14. And by the looks of the new photos, it’s hard not to think Kate and her husband, Prince William, are expanding their royal brood!

The happy couple — who married in April 2011 — would be giving a little sibling to son Prince George, 3, and daughter Princess Charlotte, 1, if the talk were true. Imagine how cute the pics of the three royal little ones would be?

Kate on Dec. 14.

However, a baby on the way for Kate and William wouldn’t be a total surprise. In September, a source close to the royal family revealed the duo are very eager to grow their family. “It would not surprise me if they were pregnant by Christmas,” the insider said.
