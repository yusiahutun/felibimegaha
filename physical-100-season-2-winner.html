Physical 100 Season 2 Winner Predictions, Who Won 2024?

 Posted on June 18, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  797 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

It’s one of the top reality shows in South Korea. If you’re keeping up with the Netflix reality survival show, here’s who could be the Physical: 100 season 2 winner. Who will come out as the Ultimate Survivor?

The competition rounds up 100 of the most physically fit people in South Korea and makes them compete in physical challenges that are either in teams or head-to-head individual competitions. Competitors include professional athletes, K-pop idols, MMA fighters, TV personalities, and more. Each person has a cast made out of their body, and once they are eliminated, they have to smash it to pieces. Only one person remains to win the fortune of ₩300 million (about US$240,000).

Though the competitions might seem that there are only physical components involved, there’s definitely some mentality to the game. “To survive, you have to pour everything into this and just keep on going,” says Jang Eun-sil to Netflix’s Tudum. “And people might double cross you. Never trust anyone. It’s full of betrayal.” Jo emphasizes a similar sentiment, adding, “Everybody’s your enemy. This is a very harsh world.”

For season 2, the competitors are put against the backdrop of a mine. Unlike the first season when we’re introduced to the contestants by them holding onto a web of bars, each contestant runs on a treadmill with all their might. Other quests include death matches where they fight one-on-one with each other and one quest where they have to transport 6 kg to 10 kg bags in a maze to offset another team.

So who will end up the Physical: 100 winner? Read more to find out.
