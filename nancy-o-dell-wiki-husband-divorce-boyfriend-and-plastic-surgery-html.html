O’Dell started her career in the field of broadcasting as a reporter as well as an anchor in her own hometown of Myrtle Beach. From then, she worked as a news anchor and later also as a crime reporter in Charleston. She was given the title of ‘Best Report of the Year Award’ by the associated press. She worked in Miami as a co-anchor alongside Jackie Nespral. Later, she left her workplace in order to move to Las Vegas with her 1st husband. She also worked as a reporter for an entertainment show called ‘A Current Affair’ until the show made an end in the year 1996.

She also worked as a lead anchor on the show called ‘Access Hollywood’ in the year 1996. She also made her contributions to News today and Dateline NBC. She has been a part of great shows like The Oscars, The Golden Globe, and The Grammys. She also co-hosted a few shows for the Golden Globes and the Emmys. In 2004 and 2005, she also hosted Miss USA and Miss Universe pageants respectively.

As her acting career, she appeared in small roles in TV series like Charmed including a premiere of season 1 and season 5. She showed up herself in Scream 2, 3 and 4 which were released in the years 1997, 2000 and 2011. She was a part of Hannah Montana’s episode of season 3 called ‘Cheat It’. She also made her appearance in the Fox series called ‘Brothers’ and also co-hosted WWE Raw on 12th of October in 2009. She was also seen participating in the 8th season of ‘Dancing with the stars’ and was paired up with the dance professional, Tony Dovolani. But later, she had to leave the show due to her knee injury occurred at the time of practice.
