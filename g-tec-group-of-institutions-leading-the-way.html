In today’s rapidly evolving world, Computer Education Brands play a pivotal role in shaping the future of individuals and societies. These centers provide a platform for acquiring essential digital skills and knowledge that have become indispensable in almost every field. From basic computer literacy to advanced programming and software development, the importance of computer education cannot be overstated. It empowers learners to navigate the digital landscape, enhances their problem-solving and critical thinking abilities, and opens up numerous opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Among the multitude of Computer Education Brands, G-TEC Group of Institutions stands out as one of the best in 2023, led by the visionary Founder, Chairman, and Managing Director, Mehroof Manalody. G-TEC has established itself as a beacon of quality learning with its unwavering commitment to excellence and a track record of impactful education.

Empowering Education for All

In the vast landscape of educational institutions, G-TEC has been making a profound impact since its inception. It is an ISO 9001:2015 certified education network founded in February 2001. With its corporate office based in Singapore and administrative office in Kerala, India, G-TEC group of institutions has established itself as a beacon of quality learning and content, spreading its global influence.

With Regional Offices in 19 countries and over 700+ learning centers, G-TEC has created a robust network reaching millions of aspiring learners. Over the years, G-TEC has proudly trained an impressive 2.2 million alumni while serving a thriving student community of 140,000+. This staggering accomplishment is a testament to G-TEC’s unwavering commitment to providing a top-notch education.

At the core of G-TEC’s success lies its extensive programs tailored to suit the diverse training needs of individuals from all walks of life. From the early stages of brain skill development for young children to comprehensive SAP Consultant training for seasoned professionals and corporates, G-TEC group of institutions offers a broad spectrum of courses in domains such as IT, Multimedia, Accounting, IT Infrastructure, Life Skills, IAS/Civil Service Training, and Entrance Coaching. This comprehensive coverage ensures that G-TEC can cater to anyone’s training requirements, empowering learners at every stage of their educational journey.

G-TEC’s commitment to quality is evident through the 18+ international certifications it has received from renowned IT Giants like Microsoft, Adobe, SAP, ACCA, Tally, Autodesk, EC Council, IAB, IIBI, University of Dubai, Mississippi State University, and many more.

This recognition has not only solidified G-TEC’s reputation as a leading institution but also garnered esteemed recognition. G-TEC group of institutions was honored with the Best International Centre award from the UK Parliament (House of Commons), a remarkable achievement for its dedication to excellence.

Building on its relentless pursuit of growth and expansion, G-TEC group of institutions recently achieved a significant milestone by acquiring an NSE-listed education network in Maharashtra. This strategic move led to forming a Public listed consortium, now known as G-TEC Jain Education Public Limited. 

Guiding G-TEC’s Visionary Success

Mehroof Manalody, from Kozhikode, Kerala, India, has played a transformative role in education. From his early days in a government school, Mehroof developed a passion for outdoor sports, particularly football, and badminton. As he pursued his Economics degree at New College, Madras, he became the college team lead in football.

Alongside his professional achievements, Mehroof cherishes spending time with his family, which includes his wife, Mrs. Vaheeda Mehroof, his daughter, Ms. Mehna Manalody, who aspires to pursue her MD after completing her MBBS, and his son, Mishal Aboobacker Manalody, currently pursuing Computer Science Engineering.

Mehroof’s impact extends beyond his family as he assumes North Kerala’s Chairman role in the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII). He also holds key positions in major trade chambers at a national level and represents India on various international business delegations. However, his role as Chairman and Managing Director of the G-TEC group of institutions has truly showcased his visionary leadership. 

Empowering Minds, Enriching Lives

G-TEC places a strong emphasis on excellence and the practical application of knowledge, ensuring alignment with industry requirements and real-world scenarios.

Pioneering IT Education for All

The growth of G-TEC group of institutions can be attributed to a pivotal turning point in the field of IT education. When the institute was founded, IT education was primarily accessible to a privileged few, with computers seen as a luxury for the elite. However, G-TEC recognized the pressing need to popularize IT and ITes (IT-enabled services) among people from all walks of life, making it affordable and inclusive.

This strategic decision triggered the institute’s rapid growth, as it addressed the demand for IT education that was prevalent in society then. Within one year, G-TEC expanded from serving a few students to catering to hundreds. By meeting the pressing need for accessible IT education, G-TEC positioned itself as a leader in the market.

Overcoming Challenges

In its early days, G-TEC group of institutions faced several challenges. At that time, computers were a luxury reserved for a small minority, and North Indian brands dominated the market. In Kerala, IT was not widely recognized as a core skill, and employers had yet to embrace IT as a necessary skill set. The absence of smartphones, limited internet access, high costs of internet facilities, and expensive computers further added to the initial hurdles. However, G-TEC foresaw the growing importance of IT and the increasing demand it would generate, especially as mobile phones began surpassing the capabilities of many computers of that era.

One significant challenge G-TEC group of institutions encountered was ensuring quality assurance and standardization across its widespread centers. Establishing a quality assurance system and maintaining standardized practices required a dedicated and skilled team. Fortunately, G-TEC overcame this challenge with the support of its exceptional team and collaboration with international certification providers. 

A Multidimensional Learning Experience

G-TEC group of institutions offers diverse courses across four skill domains, setting itself apart from other institutes. Under the brand name G-TEC COMPUTER EDUCATION, the institute delivers IT courses. G-TEC provides education through G-TEC College for Advanced Studies for undergraduate and postgraduate programs from reputed universities.

Younger students and school learners engage in courses such as Robotics, 3D printing, Abacus, and Vedic math at G-TEC Gensmart Academy. Additionally, G-TEC has partnered with approximately 800 universities in 35 countries to assist aspiring students with studying abroad under the banner of the G-TEC Global Campus.

G-TEC’s IT courses are organized into Master, PG Diploma, Diploma, and Certification programs. With over 400+ courses available, learners can explore various fields, including Software, Hardware & Networking, IT Security, Multimedia, Accounting, CAD, Fashion designing, Mobile, and English learning. What sets these courses apart is their inclusion of international certifications from renowned entities such as SAP, Tally, Microsoft, IAB, ABMA, and EC Council.

These certifications ensure the course content remains up-to-date and of the highest quality. G-TEC group of institutions has partnered with several central and state agencies, including NSDC, Keltron, NIELIT, NIOS, and Nabard.

Adapting to a Changing Landscape

Over the past decade, computer education has undergone significant transformations, reflecting the rapid advancements in technology and the changing needs of learners. Compared to a decade ago, today’s computer education system in India embraces blended learning approaches, online classes, and experiential learning, providing students with interactive and engaging educational experiences. The introduction of technology has revolutionized various aspects of life, making it more convenient, efficient, and interconnected. With the internet and readily available knowledge, information sharing has become seamless, fostering a dynamic global exchange of ideas and information.

One notable change is the remarkable progress in technology itself. Computers have become more powerful, faster, and increasingly portable, enabling users to accomplish tasks more quickly and efficiently. The advent of multi-functional devices has expanded the possibilities of modern technology, providing individuals with versatile tools for various purposes.

As technology evolves, the education system has shifted its focus from imparting purely informational knowledge to equipping students with practical skills that can be applied in real-life situations. Modern education emphasizes skill enhancement and hands-on learning, empowering students to thrive in a rapidly changing world. G-TECs’ R&D wing constantly monitors industry trends, technological advancements, and changing requirements to adapt its education and training programs accordingly. 

Empowering Graduates

G-TEC group of institutions understands the importance of providing placement opportunities and support to its students upon course completion. With the dedicated GOBS BANK department overseeing HR activities, G-TEC actively connects with corporate entities nationally and internationally. Through various initiatives such as job fairs, coordination with the center’s recruitment drive, and utilization of the institution’s job portal, G-TEC ensures that students can access various placement options.

Placing students in suitable positions is not just a corporate responsibility for G-TEC group of institutions but a fundamental commitment. Each G-TEC center has a placement officer who facilitates the placement process and assists students in securing employment opportunities. Additionally, as part of its dedication to women’s empowerment, the institution has implemented a special scheme focused on teaching and providing jobs for women, further contributing to their professional growth.
