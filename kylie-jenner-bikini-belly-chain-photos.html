Kylie Jenner Is Sizzling in an Orange Bikini and Belly Chain

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  325 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Kylie Jenner is getting an early start to bikini season this year. The cosmetics (and now fragrance) mogul recently embarked on an enviable getaway to a tropical destination, and she’s leaving fans plenty of photo-dump goodies along the way.

In an Instagram post on Monday, Jenner posed in a ruched triangle bikini awash in a vibrant blazing orange. As if the bikini weren’t eye-catching enough, the mom of two continued to accessorize with an array of luxurious jewelry pieces, including a gold watch and chain bracelet from Cartier as well as a diamond-encrusted finger bracelet from Jacquie Aiche.

But the real kicker of Jenner’s beachy look was the delicate gold studded belly chain that she wore wrapped around her midriff. The waist accessory added a little extra oomph to an already sizzling ensemble—one that is so easy to re-create yourself this summer.

Jenner also posted bikini pics on Instagram this past weekend. Seemingly fresh from a swim on a rocky beach, she looked incredibly cool in a black triangle two-piece covered in circular silver studs from Chanel, which retails for nearly $10,000.

In another post, she showed off her tropical evening wardrobe, the centerpiece of which featured a shimmery iridescent silver gown from Galvan London. The piece featured a high neckline, long sleeves, structured shoulders, and a floor-grazing hemline. At the time, she finished off the look with dewy bronze glam and a slicked-back bun.
