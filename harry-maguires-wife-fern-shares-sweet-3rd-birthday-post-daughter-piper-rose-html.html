Harry Maguire's wife Fern shares sweet 3rd birthday post for their daughter Piper Rose

 Posted on April 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  507 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Harry Maguire's wife Fern shared a sweet message for their daughter Piper's third birthday as she lifted the lid on their lavish celebrations on Tuesday. 

Taking to her Instagram story, the beauty, who also shares Lillie Saint, four, with footballer Harry, put up adorable photos to mark the occasion.

She posted a picture of herself and Harry with Piper shortly after she was born, writing: 'We love you more than you'll ever know, our precious Piper Rose.'

In another snap, Piper stood next to a wall of balloons and a light up three sign, which Fern captioned: 'My Beautiful baby girl. How are you 3?!'

She also revealed Piper's birthday cake, which was pink and decorated in the style of a fairy house.
