Just when we thought it was over, Zendaya keeps on returning for more, and once again, slaying with her fits for her Challengers press tour. In her latest look, she’s showing off her toned abs, and fans can’t cope.

The Hollywood star has been ‘serving’ lately, with her tennis-themed outfits. From her tennis ball dress to her tennis ball heels, nobody is doing it like Zendaya and her stylist, Law Roach. Her latest look may be one of her best yet, as she showed off her toned body in a Barbie-esque number. We are OBSESSED.

Zendaya shows off toned abs in chic pink number

After gracing Europe with her looks, the Euphoria star, who is currently dating Tom Holland, was back in the States, as she promoted the new movie at Amazon MGM Studios’ photo call in Los Angeles.

Sticking to the sporty theme, the 27-year-old looked stunning in a custom Jacquemus pink two-piece, a cropped polo, and a long pink skirt. The two pieces were tied together through an adorable show, leaving her midriff and toned abs on show.

The star wore her hair in a blonde bob, with effortless curls, tying the look together with a cute matching headband.

Instead of sneakers, the star opted for some cute pink pointy heels with you guessed it, a bow on the front. Perfection!

As with any Zendaya look, the pics are now going viral on X, formerly known as Twitter, and fans can’t cope, with many claiming it’s their favorite look by far. “This is my favorite one by far ahhh!” exclaimed one.

“This look WON,” said another.

“We say it’s our fave every time and that is why she will continue to be iconic,” a third said.

“OMG I LOVE THIS SO BAD,” another penned. Us too!

Serena Williams gives her ‘verdict’ on upcoming Challengers film

A new tennis film, it’s only right the GOAT of the court Serena Williams gave her opinion and that’s exactly what she did. Zendaya isn’t keeping it to herself though, as she’s spilling all the tea. Another reason we love her so much!

“She was like, ‘I know there definitely weren’t real [tennis] balls,” she told Entertainment Tonight. “She knows. She’s the best of the best.”

In the greatest compliment of all, Serena complimented Zendaya’s tennis skills as she recalled, “She did say that it was great considering that I had never, never touched a tennis ball before.”

Honestly, we wouldn’t need another compliment from anyone ever again after that.
