Singer MAX and his wife Emily are the proud parents of a baby girl named Edie

 Posted on April 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  418 words  |  Martina Birk

Lights Down Low crooner Max Schneider, better known simply as MAX, has received quite a gift ahead of the holidays.

People Magazine reports that the singer, 28, and his wife Emily Cannon welcomed a newborn baby girl on December 1st.

Named Edie Celine, the new arrival was born in Los Angeles, California, weighing in at 8 lbs., 10 oz., and measuring 21 inches long.

Proud parents: Crooner Max Schneider, better known simply as MAX, and his wife Emily Cannon welcomed a newborn baby girl on December 1st; seen here in September 2019

According to a statement from the proud parents, Edie was also born 'with a 15-inch head full of hair.'

'We are so excited to welcome our little Edie Celine Schneider into the world,' Max and Emily also said in the statement. 

'She’s got the longest eyelashes, greatest little voice and most beautiful soul. She came into the world with her eyes wide open, ready for the adventure.'

'We are so excited to welcome our little Edie Celine Schneider into the world,' Max and Emily said in a statement; seen here in January

'We couldn’t be more in love if we tried. So proud and happy to be her parents,' they added. 

Manhattan-born singer Max and wife Emily, 29, revealed they were expecting via People in July.

According to that report, the couple found out the sex of their baby the month prior, on Max's 28th birthday, which was June 21st. 

Seen here on December 1, 2019, one year before he became a dad: Manhattan-born singer Max and wife Emily revealed they were expecting in July

As it happened, that was also Father's Day this year.

Schneider and Cannon first found out they were expecting roughly a month into quarantine this year, after having tried to get pregnant 'for a long time.'

The singer, whose hits Lights Down Low and more recent Love Me Less have gained him notoriety in the US, Canada and Australia, recently serenaded his pregnant wife in the music video for his track Blueberry Eyes.
