Who Killed Sara: Netizens Agree Mexican TV Show Is Better than Spain's Elite Series

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  451 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Netflix has rolled out subsequent series of Who Killed Sara and Elite and fans of the two shows cannot keep calm
  • TUKO.co.ke held a poll to find out which of the two thriller series was top tier and netizens shared their love for their favourite show
  • Most social media users picked the Mexican series, Who Killed Sara as the best with some saying Elite disappointed them in the fourth season

Netflix has rolled out new seasons of Who Killed Sara and Elite. The new episodes have left fans picking the former as the better show.

Fans pick mightiest Spanish show

Who Killed Sara, a Mexican thriller drama series is about a man named Alex (Manolo Cardona-real name) who was framed for the murder of his sister Sara.

He gets out of prison and is unrelenting on the hunt for revenge, as he tries to uncover the mystery of what really happened.

On the other hand. Elite, A Spain original thriller series is about three working-class teens who enrol in high school, and the clash between them and wealthy students leads to murder.

Elite is currently in its fifth season and fans are looking forward to seeing the build-up, crossing their fingers that the show will not be cancelled.
