Hunter Schafer Went Topless, Wearing a Feather to the 2023 Oscars After-Party

 Posted on July 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  351 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Hunter Schafer topped all the best-dressed lists with her daring, artful look for the 2023 Oscars after-party.

The Euphoria star looked like a work of art at the Vanity Fair fete, where she wore an all-white ensemble styled by Law Roach. It was composed of a single white feather, which she wore as a bra with a sleek strap attaching it to her body. Underneath, she wore white tape covering only her nipples. Schafer paired the top with a silky white low-rise skirt that cascaded down to the carpet and white platform heels.

Her only accessories were various silver rings, and she kept her makeup to a minimum, opting for a smoky hazel eye and pink blush. Her hair was down in breezy waves.

Doug Peters - PA Images//Getty Images

"And then there was Hunter…. @hunterschafer wearing @anndemeulemeeeter by @ludovicdesaintsernin #fLAWless," Roach captioned a photo of Schafer in the stunning look on his Instagram.

The look was pulled straight from Ludovic de Saint Sernin's fall/winter 2023 runway. The designer also shared a pic of Schafer on Instagram, celebrating the achievement of having his design on the Hollywood red carpet.

Schafer spoke to Harper's BAZAAR in 2021 about her modeling career as a trans woman, her switch to TV, and her life after Euphoria's success.

"I want to have longevity as an artist, whatever form that comes in. The one thing I have faith in is the evolution of how I make art. It's something that is growing in me all the time. But there's no plan," she said. "I'm not attached to one career. I want to do everything. I'm really lucky that I have space and resources to just … try new shit and see if it sticks."
