1. L-shaped sofa

An L-shaped sofa set redefines luxury with its expansive, contemporary and sleek design. The minimalist appearance and clean lines make it an ideal fit for modern homes, plus it can also complement classic and traditional décor.

2. Recliner sofa

The recliner is another excellent piece you can purchase for your home. It exudes comfort and elegance and is available from soft leather to luxurious textiles. The recliner sofa's standout feature is its adjustable backrest and footrest that offers maximum comfort. It’ll allow you to experience warmth and relaxation while watching, reading, or working.

3. Tuxedo sofa

The Tuxedo sofa is renowned for its crisp, angular design, characterized by a uniform height for its arms and back, lending it a sleek and contemporary appearance. While not inherently conducive to relaxation due to its straight back, it serves admirably as a reading perch. But you can make it cosy using pillows.

4. Camelback sofa

The camelback sofa features a humped back that typically peaks on either side. This design usually includes gently curved arms and a sturdy wooden frame. Its versatility makes it suitable for traditional, opulent settings and contemporary interiors seeking timeless sophistication.

5. Chesterfield sofa

The Chesterfield sofa detailing sets it apart from regular sofas and gives it a distinctive look that has been stylish for years. Attractive studding, deep buttoning and rolled arms are the hallmarks of this design.

A top-quality Chesterfield sofa is more than just furniture; it's a timeless masterpiece that exudes sophistication and charm, elevating the ambience of any living space with its enduring allure and elegance.

6. Lawson sofa

Brilliant with an inviting softness, the Lawson sofa features a fold-over armrest for a contemporary, sleek design. It includes throw cushions that add an instant charm and beauty to it. A Lawson sofa works well in traditional or contemporary settings and contributes to a streamlined, clean look.

In addition, the Lawson sofa can make a great combo with a pair of English roll armchairs. However, ensure whatever pieces you use to complement the sofa are of a similar proportion to create a harmonious balance.

7. 7-seater sofa

There are several 7-seater sofa set designs, and they are ideal for people who love to entertain. The set typically comes in a 3-seater, 2-seater and two 1-seaters. It is spacious enough for friends and family and has a distinct stylish edge that looks great in different living rooms.

9. Sectional sofa

The sectional sofa is a multi-piece furniture and one of the latest sofa set designs in Kenya. You can customise it according to your space and needs. Are you looking for the best furniture for your tiny living room space? If so, you should check out the loveseat. They're slightly shorter couches, allowing two people to sit together side-by-side. As the name implies, they're ideal for couples, but anyone can use them.

11. Bridgewater sofa

The Bridgewater sofa tends to look very casual yet comfortable. Its classical design of softly rolled arms and tailored skirting invite comfort. Bridgewater sofa is a timeless piece that blends with different décor, offering a cosy and welcoming touch to any living room.

12. Armchair

When it comes to living room furniture, the armchair is definitely among the best options out there. They offer great support and comfort and come in various styles that match any décor.
