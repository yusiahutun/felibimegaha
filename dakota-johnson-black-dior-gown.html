Dakota Johnson looks incredible in a black Dior gown

 Posted on February 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  278 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Dakota Johnson, as well as being a successful actress, is known for her elegant red carpet style.

The 29-year-old had another memorable style moment this weekend, as she stepped out in a dramatic black tulle couture Dior gown.

At The Friend premiere during the Toronto International Film Festival, Johnson showed off the gown, which featured a black structured bodice and layers of tulle to create a full skirt.

GP Images//Getty Images

GEOFF ROBINS//Getty Images

The actress wore her hair tied-up in a relaxed bun, showing off her delicate jewelled necklace and matching earrings.

GP Images//Getty Images

She also wore a statement ruby ring on her finger, which was surrounded by delicate diamonds.

Post the event, Dakota made her way to a gala, where she sported an olive green strapless gown.

VALERIE MACON//Getty Images

Her second look boasted ruffles and a flowing skirt, which she paired with some silver hoops, and framed her face by pulling her hair up in to a messy bun.

She kept her make-up neutral with crimson lips, strong brows, blush pink cheeks and voluminous mascara to finish her look.

VALERIE MACON//Getty Images
