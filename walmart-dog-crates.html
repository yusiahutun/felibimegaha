Dog crates are lifesavers when you need to keep your pup secure and calm, but not every doggie is the same. Your dog’s size (or the size of your home) and needs might require different crate features. For instance, if your dog is going to snuggle up in a tight space like a bedroom, you may want a collapsable crate that stores away when he’s not using it. If you travel with your pup frequently (even just for car trips), you would want to invest in a crate that is portable with the right hardware and handles for easy carrying. Luckily, Walmart has dog crates and kennels that satisfy practically every type of dog (and owner) imaginable.

Keep scrolling for the best Walmart dog crates.

Why You Need a Dog Crate

Crates feed a dog’s natural instinct to create their own den-like space. According to the Humane Society, they are most commonly used to house-train pups and offer them a space of their own in your house. This sense of security allows them to stay home alone (eventually) and sleep train without completely destroying every shoe and piece of furniture you own. They also limit your dog’s access to the rest of your house, which is a helpful way for you to establish boundaries for your pup.
