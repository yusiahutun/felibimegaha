Ben Affleck's daughter Violet dons a face mask at star-studded fourth of July party

 Posted on July 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  528 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Ben Affleck's 17-year-old daughter Violet wore a face mask on Monday,

She joined her famous father and his equally famous second wife Jennifer Lopez, 53, at a star-studded Fourth of July party in the Hamptons neighborhood of Long Island in New York. 

The A-list party was thrown by billionaire CEO Michael Rubin. 

Violet was the only one spotted at the party wearing a face mask. However, her mask was perfectly on point with the party's mandatory all-white dress code.  

The Air star wrapped a protective arm around his daughter, who wore an elegant flowing white floor-length dress to the Independence Day soiree. 

One big happy family: Ben Affleck's 17-year-old daughter Violet joined her famous father and his equally famous second wife Jennifer Lopez, 53 at a star a white party hosted by Michael Rubin

All-star affair: The DC star's family wasn't the only group of celebs at the white party; Rubin posed with Tom Brady and other guests at his party 

The 50-year-old actor shares custody of Violet with his ex-wife, Jennifer Garner, 51. 

The Last Thing He Told Me star did not attend the the Hamptons bash, although the mother and daughter duo are frequently spotted together, indicating a close bond between the pair. 

The Daredevil star even admitted to People back in 2019 that she refused to let Violet join Instagram, fearing for her mental health.  

'When you can show me studies that say that teenage girls are happier using Instagram than not, then we can have the conversation,' Jennifer said. 

Violet still doesn't have a public Instagram page, even after attending the epic white party with her dad and stepmom.  

While the teenager is very much the spitting image of her mother, she does appear to be on good terms with her singing stepmom as well. 

The Jenny From The Block singer was spotted embracing her new stepdaughter a few weeks after she tied the knot with the Batman VS Superman star back in July 2022. 

However, the Hustlers star also respects her step-daughter's privacy by not posting photos of her on her own Instagram page with millions of followers.  

13 Going On 30: The Alias star walked to the White House hand-in-hand with her daughter to attend a state dinner for French President Emmanuel Macron; seen last December 

All grown up: Violet joined her two siblings, Samuel and Seraphina to support their mom when she was honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame; seen in 2018

Couple goals: Jen and Ben frequently make an effort to include their children on family vacations; seen in June 

In addition to being a stepmom to Violet, the Marry Me star stays busy raising her 15-year old twins Maximilian 'Max' David Muniz and Emme Maribel Muniz.

She shares custody of Max and Emme with her ex-husband, Marc Anthony. 

The business mogul echoed Jennifer Garner's concerns about fame affecting her kids during an appearance on The Tonight Show With Jimmy Fallon. 

'I think being the child of famous parents is not something many people can understand,' the Bronx-born beauty said. 

Demonstrating a wise sense of self-awareness, she added, 'I feel for [my kids] for that because they didn't choose that.'
