Jack Nicholson’s lookalike son is all loved up!

Ray Nicholson, 31, and his Portuguese model girlfriend, Sara Sampaio, 32, were spotted holding hands at a Guns N’ Roses concert in Los Angeles last Thursday, according to JustJared. Sampaio is best known for being one of the Victoria’s Secret Angels, as well as a Giorgio Armani beauty ambassador.

The stylish couple are believed to have been dating for some time now, as British media reports they were seen out and about together on numerous occasions during the summer months too.

Here’s what you need to know about the man who’s captured Sampaio’s heart:

Ray Nicholson is the son of acting legend Jack Nicholson and Rebecca Broussard

Ray Nicholson is one of Jack Nicholson’s six children. Born on February 20, 1992 to Nicholson and former actress and model Rebecca Broussard, he’s also the youngest son of the clan. Broussard shares another child, Lorraine Nicholson, with The Shining actor.

He is following in his father’s footsteps

Nicholson has pursued acting just like his Academy Award-winning father. His IMDB page shows that he has starred in films including The Benchwarmers (2006), Licorice Pizza (2021) and The Outsider (2018).

He also made an appearance in Promising Young Woman with Carey Mulligan and Bo Burnham, and Out of the Blue with Diane Kruger and Gonzo Girl with Leonardo DiCaprio’s ex-girlfriend Camila Morrone.

He’s learned not to care too much what others think of him

Nicholson isn’t afraid of what people think.

In an interview with CBR promoting Amazon Prime’s Panic TV show, he shared, “I guess [my takeaway from the show] would be sort of just don’t care so much about what people think. You can define who you are. And however the world defines you, it doesn’t matter. That’s outside of your control. You define who you are. Forget everybody else.”

He and his dad both love the LA Lakers

It’s not just a love of acting the father and son have in common.

Ray and his Batman actor dad have attended plenty of NBA games together, where they’ve been photographed cheering courtside for the Los Angeles Lakers on numerous occasions.
