FAQs

1. What is the plot of “I Saw the Devil?”

The movie follows a cat-and-mouse game between a psychopathic serial killer and a secret agent seeking revenge for the murder of his fiancée. As the agent delves deeper into the darkness, he risks losing himself in the process.

2. Is “I Saw the Devil” a violent movie?

Yes, “I Saw the Devil” is known for its graphic and brutal violence. It explores the dark depths of human nature and doesn’t shy away from depicting the gruesome acts committed by both the antagonist and the protagonist.

3. Who are the main actors in the movie?

The movie stars Lee Byung-hun as the secret agent, Kim Soo-hyun, and Choi Min-sik as the psychopathic killer, Jang Kyung-chul. Both actors deliver extraordinary performances that elevate the intensity of the film.

4. Is “I Saw the Devil” based on a true story?

No, “I Saw the Devil” is a work of fiction. While it tackles dark and disturbing themes, it is not based on any specific real-life events.

5. What makes “I Saw the Devil” stand out from other thrillers?

“I Saw the Devil” distinguishes itself through its relentless pacing, expertly crafted suspense, and its exploration of morality and vengeance. It is a film that will keep you guessing until the very end.
