Education 

The shooting guard accomplished his high school studies at South San Antonio West High School. Following it, he enrolled at the University of Texas at San Antonio. 

Career

Brown commenced playing basketball early in his life. Initially, he played for the South San Antonio West Campus High School. Back then, he became the all-time high school scorer in San Antonio.

Later, he played for four seasons at the University of Texas at San Antonio. During his tenure, Devin became the all-time leading scorer in school history. 

After his collegiate years, Devin Brown entered the 2002 NBA Draft, but he went undrafted. However, Sioux Falls Skyforce from the CBA Draft drafted him as the sixth overall pick. 

In the meantime, Fayetteville Patriots also drafted him as the second overall pick during the National Basketball Development League draft.

Professional Arena

Devin shone right through his entrance as USBL Rookie of the Year. 

In 2002, Devin bagged a contract with the Denver Nuggets, where he stayed for a year before moving to the San Antonio Spurs. 

With the Spurs, he earned his career highs and then became the NBA Champion in June 2005. The same year, he signed in with the Utah Jazz as an unrestricted free agent and stayed for a year. 

Following these, Devin Brown had a brief stint with multiple teams. He played only a year with the rest of the team, starting with Golden State Warriors, New Orleans Hornets, and Cleveland Cavaliers. 

Coming back to the Hornets in 2008, Devin then ended his career after playing with Chicago Bulls in 2010 and Asseco Prokom in 2011.

2018 DWI Arrest

In January 2018, Devin Brown was arrested on suspicion of driving while intoxicated. Well, they arrested him in the 1400 block of Loop 1604 West while he was driving his Chevy Trailblazer. 

The police stated that they were responding to a crash in that area at around 11:30 pm that involved Devin. Later, when the police approached Devin, they reported him to have a smell of strong intoxicating beverages.  

Hence, they arrested him for a DWI charge and took him to the Bexar County Jail. Devin got out in the morning at 5 am after paying $1,000. 

As a fun fact, this is not the first arrest of Brown. Back in May 2013, he was first arrested with the Class A misdemeanor drunken driving charge.
