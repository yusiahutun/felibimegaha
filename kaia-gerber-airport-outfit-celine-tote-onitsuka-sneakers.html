Kaia Gerber's Easy and Chic Airport Outfit Is Easy to Re-Create

 Posted on April 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  312 words  |  Aldo Pusey

If you’re looking for inspiration on chic and easy airport dressing, Kaia Gerber has the perfect outfit formula.

The model—who travels plenty for work and is therefore an expert on dressing for the plane—was seen at New York City’s JFK airport yesterday in a look that was casual, elevated minimalism at its best.

Amid the city’s recent rainy weather, Gerber covered up with a long, cozy black trench coat, which she left open and wore over bright cornflower-blue pants that cut off just above her ankles. For footwear, Gerber traded her go-to Adidas Sambas for the highly coveted Onitsuka Tiger Mexico 66 sneakers in black and white, which she styled with long gray socks.

BACKGRID

For her travel tote, Gerber opted for Celine’s Cabas Triomphe bag in dark brown and caramel. The It bag retails for a cool $1,950. Finally, the 22-year-old wrapped up her look with black shades, white Apple AirPods Max headphones, a gold ring, and the popular large Away carry-on in black.

She had her brown hair straight down and seemed to wear no makeup for the low-key morning.

Gerber’s look is just the latest demonstration from the model that an edited, luxury wardrobe can be anything but boring. In the past, Gerber has wowed us in a simple denim summer dress, a cool oversize suit worn with sneakers, and the perfect brown maxi dress with a subtle gold sparkle.
