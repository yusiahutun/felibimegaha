Kate Beckinsale showed off her impressive flexibility as she gave fans an insight into her fitness regime on Tuesday. 

The actress, 50, took to Instagram to share a glimpse of her private yoga class at her home on Halloween morning. 

Kate displayed her toned physique in a white sports bra and black leggings as she was coached through some tricky poses. 

The screen siren started with stretching her arms out while resting her feet by her head in the plow pose which helped stretch out the spine and hamstrings for the next move. 

With the help of her private tutor, Kate held a backbend before cooling down with the happy baby pose.

Kate got into the Halloween spirit over the weekend as she attended former TOWIE star Vas J Morgan's holiday. 

The actress revived Barbarella, the buxom space traveller made famous by Jane Fonda in the iconic 1968 film of the same name, directed by Roger Vadim. 

Dressed in the character's famous black fitted sheer jumpsuit and carrying her very own ray gun, Kate, 50, ensured she caught the eye while attending Vas J. Morgan and Michael Braun's star-studded Halloween party in Los Angeles.

In keeping with Barbarella's big screen crash landing on a faraway planet, the actress embellished her outfit with large rips and smears of fake blood ahead of her appearance at the event.

She completed the look with a pair of metallic silver platform boots that conjured images of David Bowie's Ziggy Stardust, while heavy make-up and a tousled blonde wig rounded things off.
