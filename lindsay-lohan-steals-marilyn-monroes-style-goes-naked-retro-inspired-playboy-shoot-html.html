The 32-year-old Parent Trap star looked incredible as she bent on all fours while flashing her perky posterior in the shot shared with her 7.6million followers taken from the spread from which she reportedly netted $1million (£786,000).

Lindsay's 2011 Playboy shoot set the showbiz world alight, with the legendary magazine splashing out the eye-popping sum for the spread.
