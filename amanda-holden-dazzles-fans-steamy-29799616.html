Amanda Holden has shared a steamy post-run selfie wearing a £2,500 designer necklace.

The Britain’s Got Talent star shared the sizzling snap with her followers as she returned from a jog in her workout gear, but decided to add some dazzle to her look with the pricey piece hanging around her neck.

The necklace, which was made by Van Cleef & Arpels, a luxury French jewellery company known for its “exquisite and innovative designs, high-quality materials, and fine craftsmanship".

The 52-year-old Heart FM host shared an Instagram clip of herself arriving home from her workout wearing a tiny crop top that showed off her gym-honed abs.
