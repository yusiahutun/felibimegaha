I Wear This Supermodel-Loved Unsexy Clog on Repeat and Found 8 Styles

 Posted on July 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  523 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

With each season comes a new polarizing footwear trend; I’ve hated to love and loved to hate dad sneakers, velcro sandals, and platform crocs, and this fall I’m embracing clogs with open arms. The chunky slip-ons, which are frankly so ugly they’re cute, have been around for decades, but they’re having a major moment this autumn; even Gisele Bündchen was recently spotted in a sexy rendition of the trending style.   

Not only are clogs fashionable and functional, but they also make for the perfect transitional shoe; they have the same easy to wear, slip-on feel as sandals, but with a more elevated, seasonally appropriate design. So, as fall quickly approaches, you may want to add a pair to your collection as soon as possible — trust me, they’re going to be everywhere. Luckily, Amazon is overflowing with chic, budget-friendly options starting at just $33. 

Shop Stylish Clogs at Amazon:

White Mountain Bari Clog

I would be lying if I said the White Mountain Bari clogs haven’t already made their way into my daily shoe rotation. They’re easy to throw on as I’m running out the door, and I can pair them with practically anything in my wardrobe — loungewear, jeans, skirts, you name it. They’re made from genuine leather in spite of their under $50 price tag, plus they have a contoured footbed that provides comfort and support with each step. And, I’m not the only fan of the clogs, according to their rave reviews. Many shoppers compare the shoes to pricier styles on the market; one person called them “just as good as Birkenstocks,” and someone else confirmed they’re a “great alternative” for less.   

Amazon Essentials Faux Leather Clog

When I envision the quintessential clogs, this Amazon Essentials pair is exactly what comes to mind. The classic slip-ons are the perfect addition to any outfit, since they can be made casual or easily elevated thanks to their subtle, 1.5-inch wedge heel. They’re sold in four autumnal tones and available in both faux leather and faux suede fabrics. Plus, the pair is designed with cushioned insoles and non-slip outsoles for comfortable all-day wear. The best part? They’re just $33.   

Cushionaire Heeled Clog 

If you’re in the market for a clog you can really dress up, opt for this style from Cushionaire. The pair has a chunky three-inch heel and a chic, vegan leather body. What really sold me, though, is its stylish gold-stud detailing reminiscent of the style Bündchen just wore. The shoes are sold in four versatile hues, including rich cocoa, chestnut, black, and sand. And, not only do they look great, but they’re also “comfortable right out of the box,” according to one reviewer who said they had “no break-in period.” Plus, a second shopper who works on their feet “for up to 12 hours” said they “can wear these shoes all day comfortably.”    

Be sure to head to Amazon to snag your favorite clogs ahead of fall while they’re still available for as low as $33.
