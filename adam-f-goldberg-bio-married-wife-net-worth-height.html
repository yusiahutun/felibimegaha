Who is Adam F. Goldberg? Know His Bio & Wiki

Adam F. Goldberg is born on April 2, 1976, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, the United States of America. The producer is the son of Beverly Goldberg and the late Murray Goldberg. Adam is the youngest of three siblings, with brothers Barry Goldberg and Eric Goldberg whereas he doesn’t have a sister. From the very beginning, he was interested in dramas.

In 1994, Goldberg graduated from the William Penn Charter School in Philadelphia. Then in 1998, he post-graduated from New York University after double-majoring in film and dramatic writing.

As said before Adam produced his first play when he was just 15 years old. Besides, he has already written more than fifty dramas by now. The dramas were performed around the country including various renowned theater such as the Sundance Playwrights Lab, The Greenwich Street Theater, the Illusion Theater, The Saint Marks Theatre, The Tada! Theater, the Joseph Papp Theater and The Walnut Street Theater.

After the big success of Fanboys, he was even hired to write the screenplays for The Jetsons which was the remake of Revenge of the Nerds. Adam has also produced some successful movies, including Daddy Day Camp (Columbia), The Comebacks (Fox), Bobism (MGM), Jeff the Immortal (Universal) and a remake of Night of the Living Dorks(Warner Bros).
