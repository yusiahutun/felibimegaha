What Perfume Does Rihanna Wear 2024

 Posted on October 3, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  658 words  |  Aldo Pusey

In the wake of Rihanna's groundbreaking 2023 Super Bowl halftime performance, searches for her brand Fenty Beauty spiked dramatically. Fans went into a frenzy buying her exact red lipstick shade (The MVP, if you're wondering) and the pressed powder she blotted on between hits (Invisimatte powder). But one question that has been following Rihanna for years isn't about makeup or skincare, but rather fragrance: What perfume does Rihanna wear? What does Rihanna actually smell like?

In 2021, Rihanna gave fans the chance to smell just like her. Her first scent under her Fenty brand, Fenty Eau de Parfum, sold out within hours of launch—and then again and again. After months of waiting, the perfume, with sensual notes of magnolia, musk, tangerine, and Bulgarian rose—is finally back in stock for good. It's also now available in an affordable travel-sized bottle for only $39, ideal if you want to trial it before committing. You can get all the details on Fenty's fragrance here.

Kilian Love, Don't be Shy

There were a few other hilarious clips in the thread, so be sure to click above to scroll through them all. The one question the thread didn't answer, however, is what exactly the Fenty designer smells like.

Rihanna previously had a collection of 11 perfumes with fragrance house Parlux, the first of which was released in 2010. But it was widely rumored that she didn't wear her own fragrance at the time. As first reported by Stylecaster, Rihanna's friend @StylishGent filmed a video with the singer back in 2016 in which he spills that she's wearing By Kilian Love, Don't Be Shy, a $275 perfume. Rihanna is seen in the background of the video looking totally annoyed that he's telling the world her signature scent.

By Kilian's Love, Don't Be Shy was released in 2007. It's a sweet gourmand with unmistakable notes of marshmallow, vanilla, and caramel. The top and mid notes are citrusy and white florals, but the base notes are sugary gourmands—so by the time people are smelling Rihanna in a hug, she probably smells like the world's greatest dessert. We now fully understand the commotion.
