Travis Kelce determined to keep Taylor Swift romance despite the 'mania'

 Posted on July 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  350 words  |  Martina Birk

Travis Kelce is not letting the frenzy around his new relationship get to his head as he shares his true feelings about Taylor in a new interview.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are the ‘It’ couple of 2023 but their fame comes at a huge cost. Earlier, sources close to the celebrity couple shared how the pressure on Travis’s family is taking a toll due to the pair’s extreme popularity. Being surrounded by paparazzi and an intense interest in everything Kelce allegedly made some family members uncomfortable. Yet they are said to be happy as the NFL heartthrob is happy.

Travis knows what to do for Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift’s new boyfriend Travis Kelce knows that This Love should be celebrated, not just ‘tolerated.’ The singer’s beau talked about the attention that he has been receiving since he started going out with Tay Tay, but it will not faze him.

A source close to Travis revealed: “He understands the territory their relationship comes with and like he said, isn’t letting any of the hoopla impact how they’re growing together.”

Beyond Taylor’s extreme popularity and fame, the 34-year-old NFL celebrity sees her “for who she is and vice versa.”

Travis is hooked to Taylor’s aura

Taylor Swift’s aura has made a certain Kansas City Chiefs player smitten and he is surprisingly open about it.

The sports icon talks about the impact of the music legend in his life in a new interview, saying: “But at the same time, I’m not running away from any of it…. The scrutiny she gets, how much she has a magnifying glass on her, every single day, paparazzi outside her house, outside every restaurant she goes to, after every flight she gets off, and she’s just living, enjoying life. When she acts like that I better not be the one acting all strange.”

Travis’s seriousness towards Taylor has made fans smitten, and rumors say that it will not be surprising if the duo gets engaged soon, even in the Christmas holidays as Taylor is expected to stay with her new beau in Kansas City.
