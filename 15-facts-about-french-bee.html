French Bee is a low-cost, long-haul airline based in France.

With its headquarters in Paris, French Bee operates flights to several destinations around the world, including the United States, French Polynesia, and Reunion Island.

The airline was founded in 2016.

French Bee made its debut in September 2016 and has since gained popularity for its affordable fares and quality service.

It operates a fleet of modern Airbus aircraft.

French Bee’s fleet consists of Airbus A350-900s, known for their fuel efficiency and passenger comfort.

French Bee offers a unique class configuration.

Passengers can choose between three classes on French Bee flights: “Just a Seat” for budget-conscious travelers, “Smart” for additional amenities, and “Premium” for an enhanced travel experience.

The airline is known for its vibrant yellow livery.

The distinct yellow color scheme of French Bee’s aircraft makes them easily recognizable at airports around the world.

French Bee operates flights from Paris Orly Airport.

Paris Orly Airport serves as the main hub for French Bee, providing convenient connections to various destinations.

French Bee has a strong focus on sustainability.

The airline is committed to reducing its carbon footprint and implementing eco-friendly practices within its operations.

French Bee offers a range of onboard amenities.

Passengers can enjoy in-flight entertainment, Wi-Fi connectivity, and a selection of meals and beverages during their journey.

The airline provides a generous baggage allowance.

French Bee allows passengers to check-in baggage weighing up to 23 kilograms and offers additional baggage options for purchase.

French Bee has a loyalty program called “French Bee Club”.

Passengers can join the French Bee Club to earn points and enjoy exclusive benefits such as priority boarding and discounted fares.

French Bee offers a diverse range of destination options.

From the beautiful islands of Tahiti and Bora Bora to the bustling city of New York, French Bee’s route network covers both leisure and business travel destinations.

The airline has a strong emphasis on customer satisfaction.

French Bee strives to provide excellent customer service and has received positive reviews for its attentive and friendly staff.

French Bee has partnerships with other airlines.

The airline has codeshare agreements and interline partnerships with various international carriers, allowing passengers to connect seamlessly to their final destinations.

French Bee is an expanding airline.

Since its inception, French Bee has been steadily growing its route network and increasing its passenger base.

The airline offers competitive fares.

French Bee’s commitment to low-cost travel makes it an attractive choice for budget-conscious travelers who still want to enjoy a comfortable flying experience.
