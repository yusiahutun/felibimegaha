Blake Lively gets into the Valentine's Day spirit with heart-embellished shoes

 Posted on September 28, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  208 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Getty Images

Blake Lively made it very clear this afternoon that she is big a fan of Valentine’s Day. The actress turned up to the Michael Kors show during New York Fashion Week dressed in a patent red coat, accessorised with a red bag and white shoes that were embellished with hearts.

Her look couldn't be more in keeping with the spirit of the holiday if she tried.

Getty Images

This is not the first time Lively has embraced the romantic day either. Last year, she wore a heart-print dress and styled her blonde locks into a heart-shaped updo for a Galentine’s Day party that she hosted for L’Oreal.

Getty Images

On that occasion, she also accessorised with an Anita Ko 'Cupid’s Arrow' earring that was given to her by her husband, Ryan Reynolds. Lively revealed that the actor gifted it to her a day early because he was “too excited” to wait.

"The Cupid got me very bad with him," Lively said as she explained the sentimental message behind the earring to E! News. "I have it very, very bad for my husband, so that's a nice representation of that for me."
