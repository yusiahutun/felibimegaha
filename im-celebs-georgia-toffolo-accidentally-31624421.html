I'm A Celeb's Georgia Toffolo accidentally flashes fans as boob spills out of blazer

 Posted on July 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  434 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Georgia Toffolo suffered a racy wardrobe malfunction when one of her boobs slipped out of her blazer in a hilarious video posted on Instagram.

The 29-year-old Made in Chelsea star was getting ready for a fancy agency Christmas party, so she recruited the help of her trusty followers on Instagram. However, she risked getting banned from the social media platform thanks to her racy boob-related blunder.

The 2017 I'm A Celebrity champ, who admitted she was "so stressed," showed off several stunning outfits just twenty minutes before the party was due to start, but admitted they might be too skimpy for the swanky festive do at her home.

But things went from bad to worse when she revealed her stunning black plunging black blazer with a daringly low cut and matching trousers. She went braless for the look and covered her cleavage only with a chic necklace.

It proved to be her undoing as she accidentally revealed her boob as she stepped towards the camera and hiked up her trousers which wouldn't "do up" properly. She recoiled in shock after realising she'd shown too much, crying: "Oh my god!".

Earlier on in the clip, she displayed her teeny black bubble dress, which she matched with long leather boots and a black clutch bag. Realising she could be exposing more than she bargained for, she quipped: "That's one inch away from pu** on show," before asking if she was allowed to say such a cheeky thing and bursting out laughing.

Her second look was equally as incredible, a see-through beige mesh dress, which she paired with a Dior clutch bag. Fans clearly thought she looked incredible, but they must have spotted the blunder.
