Meet Tracey Horen Sexton, Late Comedic Star Ron Sexton Wife

Comedian and Indianapolis radio personality Ron Sexton was married to his wife Tracey Horen Sexton at the time of his death. They were high school sweethearts and went to prom together. Tracey while dumping several pictures wrote in February 2017 post, “Been dating this guy for 29 years! 1988 was a long time ago!.”

The couple eventually married in August 1994. On their 21st wedding anniversary, Tracey wrote, “21 years of marriage…it hasn’t been easy, but it’s been so worth it! I love you. I love our kids. I love our life. Happy Anniversary! p.s. I deserve a prize.“

Ron, a radio host, and comedian better known to The BOB & TOM Show audience members as “Donnie Baker,” has passed away. On Saturday morning, Sexton’s family posted on his official Facebook page to share the news of his passing.

“It is with immense sadness that we let everyone know that Ron Sexton passed away yesterday. He was Donnie Baker to most of you, but Ron and Dad to us. Please respect our privacy at this time and pray for our family,” the post said. Tom Griswold on “The BOB & TOM Show” on Twitter claimed that Ron went away on Friday in Ohio while performing his stand-up comedy gig.

Ron, an Indianapolis native, has always enjoyed baseball and comedy. He was a baseball analyst for his school’s radio station during his time in high school. Sexton’s Donnie Baker made his television debut in 2005 on “The Bob & Tom Show” with stories about the yacht he seemed to be selling all the time.

He also frequently used the catchphrases “State law!” and “I swear to God” while on-screen. Along with doing impressions of famous people, Sexton also provided the voices for cartoon characters including Floyd the Trucker and Kenny Tarmac.

Ron Sexton And Tracey Horen Sexton Children

Ron Sexton and Tracey Horen shared four children together. They are named Eric, Abigail, Alex, and Aliah Sexton.

Eric their oldest is 27 years old. On his 20th birthday, Tracey wrote on her Facebook, “20 years ago today this handsome guy (not the turtle!) entered my life and made my dream come true! Eric, you make me proud every single day! Can’t wait to see what amazing things you do this year! That’s about all I can say without crying. Happy birthday to my oldest baby!.”

Eric attended Franklin Central High School and Dunedin High School. He has been working at BaseballCloud since 2018. He is in a relationship with Robin Sutton. You can find Eric on Facebook (eric.sexton.96).

Abigail is a graduate of Dunedin High School. During high school, Abigail played softball and was crowned MVP.

About Abbie, she is in a relationship. She attended Dunedin High School and Franklin Central High School. Moreover, she attended Dunedin High School and went to college at Rollins College.

Moreover, their son Alex Sexton turned 23 years old in August 2023. Alex attended Dunedin High School and played collegiate baseball at Inspiration Academy.

The youngest child Aliah Kate Sexton turned 18 years old in March 2023. On Aliah’s 10 birthday, Tracey wished her daughter, “Happy 10th birthday to our go with the flow (most of the time :)), loving, helpful, sensitive, beautiful daughter, Aliah Kate! We love you so much! Where has the time gone?“
