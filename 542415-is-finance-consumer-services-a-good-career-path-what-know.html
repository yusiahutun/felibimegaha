Types of finance consumer services

There are various types of finance consumer services available for individuals and businesses. They include;

2. Wealth management

Under this type, families, couples, or individuals are offered services for managing their wealth. The most common clients are wealthy individuals and families. Some of the services offered include:

  • Investment advise
  • Financial planning
  • Portfolio management

3. Mortgage lending

Mortgage lenders offer different types of loans to qualifying individuals and businesses to help them purchase or refinance homes. Fixed-rate and adjustable-rate mortgages are products under this type of finance consumer services.
