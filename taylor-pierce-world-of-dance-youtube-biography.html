Career:

Taylor Pierce was fond of dancing from the age of 15 and she choose to make her career in the same field. She gained her recognition as a dancer on a short video sharing platform called “Vine” because of her dancing and lip-sync videos.

Taylor Pierce being a popular dancer participated in various global dancing competitions including World of Dance. She finished at top positions in different competitions.

Taylor is also famous on the music video platform called TikTok for sharing her dancing and lip-sync videos. She gained millions of followers and likes in a very short time.

Taylor Pierce is even a video content creator. She launched her channel on 22 September 2013 and posted a debut video titled “Taylor Pierce | Blaze-Tell Me One Thing” on 23rd of September on the year 2014 which has thousands of views.

She even shares singing, dancing, choreography, pranks, reactions, lifestyle videos and travel vlogs. The most popular video on her channel is titled as “The Whisper Challenge | Marquese Scott | Taylor Pierce”.
