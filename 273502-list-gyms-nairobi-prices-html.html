List of gyms in Nairobi and prices

 Posted on March 8, 2024  |  11 minutes  |  2303 words  |  Martina Birk

Fitness has become an essential activity for the people of Nairobi not only for keeping diseases at bay but also for emotional benefits. However, the demand of a busy work schedule and the need to maintain a balanced lifestyle makes it hard to spare time to go to the gyms in Nairobi. But with the increase in health problems the importance of staying fit cannot be emphasized enough. Furthermore, nowadays you don’t have to spend hours in the gym to have a healthy body because there are many game-changing gyms in Nairobi.

Nairobi has many modern and well-equipped gyms that can cater to your unique lifestyle choices. And most of them don’t even require you to pay a hefty membership fee. So the next time you feel your fitness routine has become monotonous and is not yielding any visible results, be inspired to try one of these gyms in Nairobi.

Shani Active gym

When you enter into Shani Active gym for the first time, you will be impressed with their spacious gym which can hold up to 100 people. The equipment is modern which makes it easier for this facility to boast of having the most trending training sessions in Nairobi. From time to time this gym offers dance, martial arts, strength training, conditional training, circuit training and other fitness activities that will keep you healthy.

Kings Gym and Spa

King's gym and spa offer great value for money making it one of the best gyms in Nairobi. Located in Westlands, this gym combines fitness, exercise and well being with relaxation. Even though the equipment is modern, they are also easy to use, and in case you have any inquiries the gym instructors will be more than happy to help. After an intense body workout you can head over to the spa for a relaxing massage and to be pampered.

This gym in Nairobi also has studio classes offered by qualified and experienced instructors and can include aerobics, Zumba, spin, Pilates, body pump, Swiss ball and yoga. The prices of this gym are reasonable as in most cases you get free classes when you sign up for their fitness classes such as Zumba, boxing and karate.

Premier fitness centre

Are you looking for a clean and spacious gym to burn those calories? Then Premier fitness centre might be the gym that you're looking for. This is also one of the best boxing gyms in Nairobi. Apart from boxing, other fitness activities that you can get in this gym include indoor cycling, yoga, Pilates, cardio fitness and full body workout. The gym is easily accessible for anyone working within the CBD as it’s located along Koinange Street.

Crossfit Kwetu Gym

Located along Giri Giri lane, this gym will give you a one on one training session with a qualified instructor for only Ksh.14, 000. They are the only certified crossfit training facility in East Africa that can boast of having state of the art equipment that can mimic natural body movement.

The gym is well equipped, clean and spacious. You can choose from a wide array of exercise and fitness routines which include gymnastics, body weight training, Olympic weightlifting, swimming. Samto fitness centre

Samto fitness centre is one of the most trusted gyms in Nairobi West. With more than 1,000 active members, this gym has qualified instructors to help you become healthier, energetic and more active in your day to day life. This gym prides itself on offering lively fitness classes such as Tae Bo, Zumba, Aero Step aerobics and Aero-Toning. Apart from offering fitness classes, this gym has every type of training equipment you can imagine including heavyweight dumbbells, cardio machines and cross cable machine where you can do multiple exercises.

The gym is open throughout the week and has a juice bar and massage parlor for when you need to refresh yourself. The prices for Samto differ depending on the type of membership you want. The membership prices which are designed to suit your lifestyle are divided into five groups:

A daily plan; a monthly planned called Afro plan, the waka waka plan which has a 3-month payment plan, the half a year plan called Zumba plan and the Samba which is a full year payment plan. Regardless of which plan you prefer you will still get access to all the facilities in the gym, towel services, instructors, aerobic classes and ample parking with adequate security. With all these services, the Samto Fitness Centre offers great value for your money.

Racing Sports Gym Westlands

It’s impossible to give a list of gyms in Nairobi without mentioning Racing Sport gym. Their prices are affordable, they facility is clean, the changing room is fancy, the staff are friendly, and they have a variety of equipment that you can choose. Racing Sports gym closes at 10 pm which gives working individual ample time to exercise and keep fit. You can use this gym during their off-peak hours of between 8 am to 5 pm for which you will pay a monthly charge of Ksh.3,990.

With more than 15 years of gym experience, the instructors and all staff run this facility with the passion for helping you get the type of body shape you want. While the parking is free during your workout duration, to access the gym and all the services they have to offer, you have to pay a onetime membership subscription of Ksh.500 and an annual subscription of Ksh.55,000 or Ksh.38, 900 for off-peak hours. Other prices that you can opt to pay are a three-month membership subscription of Ksh.15, 500 or a one month price of Ksh.5, 500.

Parklands Sports Club gym

The gym at Parklands Sports Club Nairobi is open from 4 am to 9 pm and has various equipment and classes that you can use to maintain your healthy lifestyle. This gym offers different aerobic sessions such as low impact aerobics, high-intensity aerobics and circuit aerobics. The well-trained instructors can help you with marathon classes, power yoga, spin classes, circuit training, Rumba, Songa dance and high explosive training.

The prices for accessing this gym are reasonable. And when you opt to become a member of the Parklands Sports Club Gym, you will also get access to a wide range of cardio machines, cable machines, dumbbells, kettlebells and plate loaded machines. The instructors will also monitor your progress and push you to achieve your goal.
