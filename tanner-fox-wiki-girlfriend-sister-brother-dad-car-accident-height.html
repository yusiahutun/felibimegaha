Tanner Fox is a vlogger who runs his own YouTube channel and is a professional stunt scooter rider who was born on December 22, 1999, in San Diego, California. His fascination with training scooters and hoverboards began in his childhood.

Tanner started skateboarding when he was just 2 years old. Also uploading videos on the internet was his passion from childhood on. Tanner Fox depended solely on homeschooling, which lasted only two days. He used to read in a private Catholic school, where he was eventually expelled from school for making videos during lessons. The reason for his expulsion eventually became the reason for his fame.

Tanner Fox Wiki

Tanner Fox is the creator of the YouTube channel MT-Filme, which is developing rapidly and has attracted many viewers since its inception. His channel offers challenging and inspiring videos and scooter stunts. Tanner’s favorite stunt, which he likes to perform, is the freestyle flair category. His love and dedication to his scooter stunts have contributed a lot to his successful career. Several of his videos have also featured his best friend Jake Angeles, who is just as passionate as he is about scooter riding.

Homeschooling has helped him to develop his scooter skills as it gives him more time to spend with his friends. Tanner is inspired by Dylan Morrison, the famous scooter rider who is known for his extraordinary and funny stunts on his vehicle.

Tanner is also associated with particularly popular games like Root Industries, Lucky Scooters, and Grind shop and has made several video demos. He has had the opportunity to work with the popular Roman Atwood and even had a test drive in his GTR car. He has also been associated with various pros in the scooter driving career such as Casey Neistat and Justin Stuart. They all helped in the development of his career. At some point, Fox was featured in a YouTube video entitled BMX vs. SCOOTER with Harry Main.

Youtube, Social Media & Brands

It currently has over 6 million Youtube subscribers. His main audience happens to be teenagers, and he has over 3.4 million followers on Instagram.

Tanner Fox has a very active brand and sells branded t-shirts, sticker packs and hats on the tfoxbrand.com website. He enjoys spending time with his dog Kirby and has even set up an account for the dog that is regularly maintained. Tanner spends his free time with his family and friends. His favorite place in the world, which he likes to visit, is Barcelona, Spain.

Tanner Fox Girlfriend

There were rumors that Tanner had a girlfriend named Anna in the past, who was a high school friend, but there was no official confirmation of this fact. However, sometime around April 2017, Tanner tweeted that he wanted a girlfriend with whom he could have a romantic time. He later confirmed that he had a relationship with Taylor Alessia, with whom he went to Disneyland, and you could see them kissing.

Sister and Dad

Tanner has an older sister named Lindsay Fox. She grew up with Tanner in San Diego, California. Lindsay is also a Youtube and Instagram sensation and has made a name for herself as Tanner’s sister. Her first videos on Youtube were “Exposing my brother” and “Sibling Tag with Tanner Fox”.

Not much is known about his father, but in one of his videos, Tanner introduced a man named Steve as his father. Later he made clear that Steve was the friend of his mother.

Tanner Fox Cars and Accident

In August 2016, Tanner got his dream car – a 2010 Nissan GTR, and in September 2016 he changed the car’s exhaust and got new ones that were louder than the original parts.

In November 2016 he released a video in which he was seen with another car he had received in exchange for his Nissan GTR 2010. In the video, he explained that he had his car checked and received a good offer. It was a 2017 Nissan GTR Premium, which he received in exchange for his 2010 Nissan GTR.

In the same month, Tanner and his friends Maverick and Danny were involved in a car accident. They were driving Maverick’s car, a 240SX, when they lost control and the car went off a cliff. They all came out alive. Tanner had a split chin, a broken leg, and marks on his shoulders. Later he had surgery on his leg.

Measurements

Tanner Fox has a height of 172 cm (5 feet and 8 inches) and a weight of 62 kg.
