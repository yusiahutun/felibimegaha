Martha Stewart Wine - We Challenged Martha Stewart to a Game of 'Never Have I Ever...&quot;

 Posted on March 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  375 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Martha Stewart is an expert on all things entertaining, etiquette, food, wine–and, as we discovered, drinking games–particularly those that involve wine from her very own range, Martha Stewart Wine Co. After a few sips of 2016 Les Muraires Coup de Rosé, the world's best known hostess got real on kissing on the first date, showing up to a red carpet tipsy, smoking with her co-host, Snoop Dogg, and double booking two dates on the same night.

Per Martha, kissing on the first date is the best way to cut through the BS and get to know someone–fast. Everyone double dips; shareable drinks are "disgusting," and the phrase "Martha Stewart pirogies" has more traction on Google than "Martha Stewart." Oh, and Match.com and other dating sites just don't write algorithms to match people like Martha–she's a complicated date.

One more tip from the reigning queen of entertaining and living well: there is no shame in re-gifting a bottle of wine. But why not insure you're buying a wine you'll love to drink, and that, upon gifting, nobody ever wants to pass off? We tapped Martha for her wine recommendations for easy drinking, gifting, and should the mood ever strike, for playing a quick game of "Never Have I Ever..."
